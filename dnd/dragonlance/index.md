# Dragonlance: Shadow of the Dragon Queen - Reference Guide

## Key Characters & Factions

- **Ispin Greenshield:** A beloved, late adventurer known across Solamnia for his signature green shield and tall tales. His death serves as the initial catalyst for the party's gathering.
- **Becklin Uth Vaharin:** A noble Knight of Solamnia and old companion of Ispin, stationed at Thornwall Keep in the village of Vogler.
- **Darrett Highwater:** Becklin’s dedicated squire and later the party's primary military liaison officer within the Kalaman armed forces.
- **Cudgel Ironclad:** The fierce leader of the Ironclad Regiment, a mercenary company initially hired for festival re-enactments who later becomes a core tactical ally.
- **The Dragon Army (Red Wing):** A brutal, highly organized invading force marching under the banner of the Dragon Queen, utilizing reptilian Draconians, Hobgoblins, and terrifying siege machinery.
- **Dalamar the Dark:** A mysterious and ambitious elven mage encountered in the Northern Wastes, seeking magical secrets while offering critical tactical intelligence to the party.

## Magical Inventions & Artifacts

- **The Boilerdrak:** A terrifying, dragon-shaped siege engine engineered by gnomes but corrupted by the Dragon Army to unleash devastating torrents of flame.
- **The Gnomeflinger:** A chaotic, high-powered gnomish catapult designed for rapid personal transport or improvised defense.
- **The Fargab:** A highly sophisticated, experimental gnomish communication device allowing long-distance vocal transmission.
- **The Dragonlance:** A legendary, divine weapon forged specifically to combat dragons and their kin, recovered and repaired by the party over the course of the campaign.

## Regional Geography

- **Vogler:** A tranquil, riverside fishing village that serves as the tragic opening stage of the Dragon Army's invasion.
- **Kalaman:** A heavily fortified port city, serving as the central bastion of defense and military command in the region.
- **The Northern Wastes:** A barren, treacherous expanse of scarred earth, ancient ruins, and lethal wildlife hiding the path to the lost past.
- **The City of Lost Names (Onyari):** A legendary, ruined flying city of pleasure built atop the remains of dead dragons, acting as the ultimate objective for both the party and the Dragon Army.
