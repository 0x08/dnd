# Session 2: The Coming Storm

Following the harrowing betrayal at the Battle of the High Hill, the party's heroic efforts pushed them to new heights, and you officially attained level 3!

The group immediately set to work administering medical aid to the wounded villagers before securing one of the surviving traitorous mercenaries for a stern interrogation. Under your fierce questioning, the captive cracked and spilled his secrets. He revealed that Gregaris had ventured into a dense forest west of town a few days prior, returning with a massive iron coffer overflowing with gold coins. Using this sinister hoard, Gregaris had successfully bribed a large faction of Cudgel's men, ordering them to slaughter the town militia during the re-enactment as a precursor to a full-scale raid on the defenseless village.

Armed with this terrifying intelligence, the party marched directly toward the western forest to investigate. It did not take long to discover heavy tracks cutting north through the mud. Following the trail, you climbed a ridge and were horrified to behold a massive, heavily armed military host marching under an unknown banner. Before you could retreat, an advanced army scout patrol consisting of six fierce, reptilian creatures spotted you. A swift skirmish broke out, and the party successfully eliminated the patrol before sprinting back to Vogler to sound the alarm.

You immediately briefed the town council on the encroaching invasion. Cudgel returned shortly after, escorting a loyal mercenary captain named Jeyev, and both grimly confirmed that they had spotted the exact same invading army advancing through the wilderness. Moments later, a breathless messenger arrived at the River Gate bearing a formal ultimatum from the attackers: they were the vanguard of the Dragon Queen's Red Army, and they commanded the village to surrender and prepare to receive their occupying forces by nightfall.

Recognizing the desperate need for heavy military reinforcement, Becklin’s squire, Darrett, bravely volunteered to take a fast skiff down the river to Kalaman in a high-stakes bid to alert the grand marshal. Not long after his departure, the vanguard of the Dragon Army arrived, taking up threatening positions atop the ridges and just outside the main gates.

Utilizing a highly volatile gnomish contraption known as the Gnomeflinger, the party launched themselves across the lines directly into the mercenary camp. Through a brilliant display of tactical deception and quick-witted diplomacy, you managed to guide the remaining loyal mercenaries safely inside the village walls to reinforce the perimeter. Meanwhile, Dave took charge of the civilian population, meticulously organizing a massive evacuation effort to ferry the terrified villagers across to the safety of the river's north bank. In a quiet, heavy moment amidst the preparations, Becklin pulled you aside to confide that she had hidden a secure box beneath her bed back at Thornwall Keep; she made you swear that if she should fall in the coming battle, you would retrieve the box and deliver it directly to Darrett. With the enemy beating at the gates, Becklin, Cudgel, and the remaining mercenaries marched to the north gate to mount a desperate final defense, leaving the party to oversee the final phase of the civilian retreat. Seeking to maximize your defenses, you paid one last visit to the local inventor, Than, instructing him to calibrate and utilize the Gnomeflinger as a heavy piece of artillery should the defenses crumble.
