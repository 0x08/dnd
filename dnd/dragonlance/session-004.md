# Session 4: The Special Taskforce of Kalaman

Following a long, cold night drifting down the river, your ragged fleet finally reached the shores near the grand fortified city of Kalaman. The coastline was a scene of utter despair; hundreds of Vogler refugees were dragging their crude fishing boats onto the mud, desperately attempting to spark campfires, gather food, and erect makeshift shelters against the elements. Amidst the chaos on the shoreline, you successfully reunited with Darrett, who was completely devastated to learn that his noble mentor, Becklin, had perished defending the gates. He informed you that while he had established initial contact with the Kalaman leadership, the high council was extremely eager to interview the party directly to gather firsthand intelligence on the invading force.

Once you ensured that the refugees were as stable and comfortable as possible under the circumstances, you marched into the city alongside Darrett, heading directly for the imposing spires of Kalaman Castle. Upon entering the grand audience chamber, you were highly annoyed to find that the manipulative Lord Bakaris and his arrogant son had already beaten you to the city, actively whispering in the ears of the leadership to push their own selfish political agendas.

The party stepped forward to brief the council, addressing the Governor, Calof Miat, the local Guild Leaders, and the stern Grand Marshal, Nestra Vendri. Impressed by your military competence and desperate for actionable intelligence, the council agreed to officially take in the Vogler refugees and provide them with proper municipal lodging—on the strict condition that the party enlist as a specialized military taskforce dedicated to high-stakes special operations against the Dragon Army threat. You accepted the terms, were assigned private quarters within the castle barracks, received a handsome advance in pay, and were granted official military insignias identifying your elite status. To your delight, Darrett was officially designated as your primary military liaison officer to manage your upcoming assignments.

With coin in hand, the party spent the afternoon conducting an extensive shopping and weapon-upgrading excursion at the famous Twin Hammerstrike Forges. During the break, Boycott successfully channeled a secret magical recipe passed down from his beloved Nannan, casting a sophisticated ritual spell to permanently summon a beautiful celestial spirit in the form of a helpful owl.

The following morning, Darrett delivered your very first official deployment. The military leadership was deeply alarmed by your reports of the fire-spewing Boilerdrak and desperately wished to analyze its design. You were ordered to seek out an eccentric gnomish engineer named Tatina Rookledust, who resided in the nearby hills and was rumored to be a foremost expert on such experimental machinery.

The party loaded into a horse-drawn supply cart and trotted out into the countryside. However, as you approached Rookledust’s workshop, you found her hilltop estate completely overrun by a raiding party of vicious Goblins. The party deployed rapidly from the cart, swiftly dispatching the raiders with practiced efficiency before knocking on the laboratory doors. Upon meeting Tatina, the quirky inventor was profoundly saddened to learn that her peaceful invention—which she had originally designed to function as an automated lawnmower—had been weaponized by the Dragon Army to burn down a village. Overjoyed by your rescue and eager to assist the war effort, she happily packed up her tools to accompany you back to Kalaman, presenting the party with her latest prototype communication device: a pair of miraculous, long-distance vocal transceivers known as the "Fargab." Together, you loaded her gear into the wagon and began the journey back to the city walls.
