# Session 6: The Infiltration of Wheelwatch Outpost

The next morning, Darrett gathered the party around the war table to deliver a grim update: fresh intelligence received at dawn confirmed that Wheelwatch Outpost had just been completely overrun and occupied by the vanguard of the Dragon Army. The high command officially ordered the Benebog Slayers to march out and liberate the strategic stone fortress, operating in perfect coordination with the surviving remnants of Cudgel’s loyal mercenaries.

During the journey out to the frontier, Cudgel rode alongside the party, providing a meticulous, room-by-room breakdown of the fortress's architectural layout and defensive weak points. Upon nearing the objective, you carefully steered your supply carts off the road, concealing them deep within the thick brush roughly one mile from the fortress walls to avoid detection.

Studying the battlements, you opted to implement a daring, two-pronged tactical assault. The party split into two specialized teams: an elite infiltration squad consisting of Blip, Boycott, and Snub would attempt to scale the walls under the cover of darkness to seize the gatehouse, while a heavy assault force led by Dave and Cudgel would wait in the tree line, prepared to storm the courtyard the exact moment the main gates were swung open.

The infiltration team executed their climb flawlessly, silently dropping onto the stone ramparts. They immediately crossed paths with a handful of sentries, eliminating them quietly before they could raise an alarm. However, the plan hit a major snag when they struck down a high-ranking Draconian sentry; upon its death, the creature’s dark magic triggered a screaming, auditory illusion that echoed across the battlements, threatening to alert the entire garrison. Thinking incredibly fast, Snub utilized brilliant roleplay and a cunning vocal deception to completely fool the approaching guards, convincing them it was a false alarm. With the garrison pacified, the trio methodically swept through the western side of the fortress, quietly dispatching every single enemy in the sector.

With the area temporarily secured, a disguised Snub embarked on a daring solo tour of the fortress interior. He successfully mapped the central structure, spotting an oblivious officer busy drafting a military report and locating a pair of mistreated prisoners locked in the cells. Slipping into the officer's barracks, Snub successfully looted a cache containing sixty gold pieces along with the master heavy iron key to the main gate mechanism.

Moving swiftly to the gatehouse, the infiltration team turned the heavy iron winches, throwing the massive wooden gates wide open. Dave, Cudgel, and the wave of loyal mercenaries came flooding out of the tree line like an avalanche, spilling into the courtyard and completely overwhelming the occupied fortress. The garrison was entirely put to the sword, save for a single, terrified soldier whom the party chose to disarm and capture for later interrogation.
