# Session 9: The Catacombs of Sarlamir

Following the faint, pulsing violet illumination, the party cautiously descended the ancient stone staircase into the deep catacombs stretching far beneath Kalaman Castle. As you ventured through the damp, subterranean crypts, the swirling violet mist flared to life, forcing the party to witness a series of vivid, historical illusions depicting the tragic life story of the legendary death knight, Lord Soth. The exploration was violently interrupted as a pack of malevolent wraiths materialized from the stone sarcophagi, but the party coordinated effectively and swiftly banished the spirits.

Marching deeper into the complex, you discovered a series of massive, jagged holes that had been brutally blasted directly through the ancient reinforced walls of the catacombs, all heading in a definitive southward trajectory. Amidst the debris of a forgotten vault, the party uncovered a trove of rare historical equipment, including a *+1 longsword*, a pristine steel breastplate, and a beautifully woven Kagonesti Forest Shroud made entirely of living leaves. Dave spent some time interacting with a pair of ancient warhorse skeletons resting in an alcove, successfully earning a divine blessing known as the "Favor of the Heroic Steed."

The party eventually discovered a hidden subterranean shrine, where you crossed paths with a peaceful spectral spirit who introduced herself as Jandin. She shared deep historical lore regarding the legendary Knight Sarlamir, explaining how his pride, ultimate disobedience, and wicked deeds during the age of the Cataclysm had brought a permanent curse upon his bloodline. She noted that the eerie, supernatural flames dancing across the tombs were the literal leftover spiritual embers of that ancient divine wrath.

The group pressed forward into the grand central tomb of Sarlamir himself. The moment you crossed the threshold, you were pulled into a powerful historical vision: you watched a spectral Lord Soth approach a roaring brazier, touching the flames with a dark scepter carved with screaming faces. Suddenly, the vision shattered as the skeletal form of Lord Sarlamir rose furiously from his stone sarcophagus to attack. A fierce boss battle ensued, during which the undead knight bellowed about Lord Soth, claiming the death knight had beckoned him to march back north to the legendary "City of Lost Names"—the precise site of his historical dishonor.

With a final, coordinated strike, the party defeated Sarlamir, causing his bones to turn to ash. The very moment he was laid to rest, the supernatural violet flames dancing across his weapon and throughout the catacombs instantly vanished. Searching the empty sarcophagus, you recovered a singular, heavy relic: the rusty iron head of an ancient spear.

This monumental victory pushed the party's capabilities to an elite status, and you all gained a level, officially reaching level 6!

Realizing that Lord Soth had already escaped the catacombs long before you arrived, you ascended back to the surface. You found that Darrett had just arrived back within the city walls alongside Marshal Vendri, Lord Bakaris, and the bulk of the Kalaman army. Marshal Vendri immediately moved to establish strict martial law to keep order in the panicked city, while a fascinated Darrett pulled the party aside, deeply eager to learn every single detail regarding Lord Soth and the mysterious City of Lost Names.
