# Session 11: The Obelisk of Wakenreth

The journey toward the Blue Phoenix Shrine was filled with academic debate. As you traveled across the desolate landscape, Dalamar chatted extensively with the group, analyzing old ruins and trying to gauge whether any of you possessed a genuine passion for ancient history. Blip seemed to take a strong, immediate liking to the charismatic elven mage, and the two spent the march getting along remarkably well.

Upon nearing the sacred perimeter of the shrine, the party crossed paths with a small band of displaced Sea Elves. Their vanguard leader introduced himself as Ishvern Stargazer, explaining that his people were attempting to complete their traditional annual pilgrimage to the temple. Tragically, they had been ambushed by a advance force of the Dragon Army, and their high priestess, Yearkal, had been captured and dragged away into the wastes. Ishvern passionately pleaded for the party's assistance in completing their sacred offering, promising a handsome reward for your protection.

Ishvern accompanied the group inside the Blue Phoenix Shrine, guiding you through the weathered architecture, though he admitted he knew very little of the specific rituals as Yearkal had always handled the spiritual mechanics. The party pushed into the absolute inner sanctum of the temple, reverently placing their offering of shimmering blue pearls upon the ancient altar. In recognition of your piety, the altar flared with divine light, and Ishvern rewarded each party member with a matching blue pearl. Suddenly, a warm sensation washed over the group as you received Habbakuk’s divine blessing, permanently bolstering your survival capabilities as you traveled through the harsh Northern Wastes. As you exited the shrine, a sea elf scout came racing back from the desert, reporting that they had successfully tracked the captured priestess to a fortified enemy outpost known as Camp Carrionclay, located near the murky waters of Sky's Tears.

Wishing to secure the remaining marked locations before launching a rescue mission, the party altered their trajectory and marched directly toward the ancient obelisk of Wakenreth. Arriving at the site, you were stunned by a magnificent display of broken magic: massive stone blocks were floating perfectly locked in place in mid-air, levitating around the perimeter of a broken tower.

The party stepped through the threshold of the mystical tower, discovering a grand central chamber dominated by a massive stone statue of an elven warrior surrounded by hundreds of glinting silver coins. Seizing the financial opportunity, Dave spent twenty minutes methodically picking up every single piece of silver on the floor.

Ascending a spiral staircase to the second level of the tower, the party was suddenly blindsided by two vengeful wraiths. A fast, frantic battle followed; once you defeated the spirits, the ghost of a grey-haired woman detached herself from the ectoplasm and floated gracefully up through the ceiling to the chamber above, beckoning the party to follow her.
