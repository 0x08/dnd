# Session 12: The Dancing Longsword and the Worm

Following the ghostly apparition up to the third level of the Wakenreth tower, the party found themselves face-to-face with a peaceful, floating spirit. The phantom identified herself as Jentida, explaining that she had originally served as a guard for a historical elven expedition sent to the wastes centuries ago. She anxiously inquired if the party had ever crossed paths with her sister, Tenadria, a renowned elven diplomat in the civilized lands. Recognizing the party's noble intent, Jentida floated over to Dave and formally presented him with her ancient family heirloom: a magnificent, magical *Dancing Longsword*.

The party continued their ascent to the absolute apex of the Wakenreth tower, discovering a swirling, highly volatile magical portal humming at the center of the platform. Driven by reckless curiosity, Dave stepped forward and began tampering with the portal controls multiple times. His interference destabilized the energy field, causing a massive, extraplanar monstrosity to tear through the rift and attack. A frantic, high-stakes battle ensued on the high platform, but the party fought with perfect cohesion, ultimately slaying the beast and successfully sealing the rift. With the portal safely closed and the tower's levitating architecture permanently restored to structural stability, Dalamar expressed a desire to remain behind to conduct a meticulous, long-term investigation of the local runes.

The remaining party members packed their gear and set out across the desert toward the final marked location: the Sunward Fortress. However, as you navigated a series of deep sand dunes, the earth suddenly shattered beneath your feet as a colossal, ravenous Purple Worm burst from the ground to ambush the group. The combat was terrifyingly brutal; multiple party members were swallowed whole and ingested into the monster's acidic stomach, but through desperate internal strikes and heavy external magic, you managed to slay the titanic worm and drag your battered companions out of its carcass.

Wiping the worm slime from your armor, you pressed forward through the wastes, finally arriving at the gates of the Sunward Fortress, where the vanguard of the Kalaman army was waiting anxiously for your arrival.
