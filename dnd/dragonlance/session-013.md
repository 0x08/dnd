# Session 13: Slaadi In the Deep

The party remained deployed deep within the dangerous expanses of the Northern Wastes, desperately trying to uncover the Red Dragon Army’s ultimate intentions. As you recall, a massive faction of the enemy host had inexplicably marched into this barren wasteland, and your taskforce was specifically assigned to determine what they were looking for. With your archaeological companion Dalamar still hard at work deciphering runes back at Wakenreth, the party gathered around the command table at the Sunward Fortress to brief Darrett and Cudgel on your findings. After a brief tactical discussion, it was decided that the party would head down into the fortress's massive subterranean excavation site to investigate.

Approaching the dark entrance of the cavern complex, you discovered a pristine, glowing pool of mystical fluid. After offering a collective prayer to the gods of good, the liquid resonated, and you successfully scooped up several vials of potent *fire breath liquid*.

Descending into the damp, excavated tunnels, the party was immediately ambushed by a squad of grotesque, mutated Draconians. A fierce, bloody skirmish followed, during which you successfully eliminated the mutations. However, during a post-combat medical sweep, you made a horrifying discovery: Boycott had been thoroughly infected with an alien parasite by a Slaad, and the rest of the party had to work quickly using daggers to painfully excise a cluster of squirming alien eggs nestled just beneath the surface of their own skin.

Pushing deeper into the subterranean tunnels, you discovered a hidden holding cell and successfully rescued the captured sea elf priestess, Yearkal, while administering vital healing magic to a severely battered local guide named Rone. After helping them to their feet, you escorted the survivors back up to the surface to the safety of the army lines. Deeply grateful for his salvation, Rone offered the party a staggering reward of five hundred gold pieces on the strict condition that you escort him safely back to his hidden home village of Heart's Hollow.

The party returned to the depths to complete the purge of the cavern complex. Slipping into the lowest chamber, you engaged and defeated a vicious pack of fully grown Slaadi. Hidden behind their nesting grounds, you uncovered the ultimate prize: a massive, glowing orange crystal pulsing with raw, volatile magical energy. Through these harrowing subterranean trials, the party's combat mastery reached an elite threshold, and you officially achieved level 7!
