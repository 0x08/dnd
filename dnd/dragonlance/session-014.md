# Session 14: Arrival at Heart's Hollow

The party continued their dangerous expedition across the Northern Wastes, driven by the firm belief that locating the mythical City of Lost Names held the ultimate clue to the Dragon Army's presence in the region. Back at the obelisk, Dalamar had sent word via a sending spell that he believed he could pinpoint the exact coordinates of the ruined flying city by systematically translating the writings on the tower walls.

Before departing the Sunward Fortress, the party conducted a final, meticulous sweep of the deep cavern where you had slain the warlord Viruzza. Your search yielded a fascinating trove of rare items, including a handful of pulsing orange stones, a powerful *wand of the war mage*, and a bundle of handwritten military dispatches. Reading through the captured notes, the horrific agenda of the Red Dragon Army was finally laid bare: they were utilizing the massive, glowing orange crystal in the cave to forcefully mutate innocent captives, transforming them into loyal Draconian soldiers. Suddenly, Snub stepped forward and affectionately hugged the massive orange stone; he immediately fell violently ill, and over the next forty-eight hours, his physical condition deteriorated rapidly as a supernatural sickness took hold of his body.

Ascending to the surface, you held a final briefing with Yearkal and Rone. Yearkal was deeply intrigued to hear of your alliance with Ishvern's pilgrimage, but she tearfully requested that you keep an eye out for several more missing elves who were still unaccounted for. Rone reiterated his desperate plea, offering five hundred gold pieces to act as a security escort to his secret, subterranean sanctuary of Heart's Hollow.

The party loaded Rone into a supply cart and set off across the wastes, with Snub growing increasingly feverish and pale in the back of the wagon. On the second day of travel, you successfully spotted and ambushed a Red Dragon Army desert patrol, wiping them out completely before they could raise an alarm.

After two grueling days of travel through the canyons, you finally neared the coordinates of the hidden settlement. Just as you approached a massive geological formation known as The Crevasse, you heard frantic shouting; a local scavenger named Clystran was sprinting for his life, pursued by five monstrous Giant Spiders. The party deployed instantly from the cart, slicing through the arachnids with practiced efficiency. Overjoyed by your timely intervention, a breathless Clystran explained that he had lived in the hidden village of Heart's Hollow his entire life, and he would be absolutely delighted to guide you and Rone safely through the secret mountain passes. Following his lead down a series of concealed tunnels, the party finally stepped out into the breathtaking, subterranean oasis of Heart's Hollow!
