# Session 15: The Secret of Bluemaw Cave

Upon entering the beautiful, hidden sanctuary of Heart's Hollow, your first priority was escorting the wounded Rone safely back to his family home. Upon arrival, his mother embraced her son tearfully and presented the party with a reward of four hundred gold pieces—noting with an apologetic grimace that it was exactly one hundred gold less than originally agreed due to their current financial hardships. To make amends, she treated the entire famished taskforce to a massive, steaming shepherd's pie, proudly declaring it to be the ultimate culinary specialty of Heart's Hollow.

With your bellies full, you immediately carried the shivering, delirious Snub down to the bottom of the town's grand Mosaic structure to see Lavender, a highly eccentric local mage and potion brewer. After analyzing Snub's condition, Lavender suggested a casting of *lesser restoration*, which successfully purged the orange crystal toxins and restored the rogue to perfect health. Seizing the opportunity, the party spent a portion of their reward money to purchase a handful of rare alchemical mixtures from her shelves, including several valuable *potions of clairvoyance*.

Later that afternoon, the party approached the central town notice board to scout for local contract work. You located a prominent flyer detailing a family of desperate Dwarves who were in dire need of immediate military assistance, with instructions to interview the local innkeeper to gather the specifics. The innkeeper revealed that a clan of dwarves had been forcefully driven out of their ancestral home at Bluemaw Cave by invading forces.

The party gathered their gear and marched out toward the coordinates of Bluemaw Cave. As you neared the dark cavern entrance, Snub uncorked and drank one of his newly acquired *potions of clairvoyance*, granting him a vivid, magical mental layout of the entire cave structure. Pushing into the shadows, you narrowly avoided stepping directly into a deadly mechanical trap. Suddenly, a dwarf woman named Grasha stepped out from the tunnels, sincerely apologizing for almost blowing you up. She explained that she had laid the traps to combat a raiding party of Dragon Army Draconians who had been methodically looting the cave, searching for the legendary lost hoard of a long-gone blue dragon. The party made a swift contractual agreement: you promised to completely eradicate the invaders in exchange for the remainder of the dragon's treasure hoard.

The group advanced deep into the cavern chambers. You first encountered and sliced through a ravenous pack of subterranean Gricks before cornering the looting Draconians in the main vault. The party unleashed a devastating barrage of high-level *fireball* spells, completely incinerating the enemy forces and securing the cavern.
