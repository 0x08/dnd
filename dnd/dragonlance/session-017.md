# Session 17: The Road to Onyari

**Date:** September 20, 2025

The aftermath of the total destruction of Camp Carrionclay yielded an incredible series of triumphs. Pushing into the command tents, the party discovered a massive, unhatched dragon egg resting in an arcane incubator. Moments later, you smashed the iron locks on the slave cages, successfully liberating the captured Sea Elves alongside a joyous band of captive Kender. You also managed to free a captured, massive Bulette beast from its training pins. Suddenly, a terrifying adult Black Dragon descended from the storm clouds, landing in the center of the camp. Showing incredible bravery and quick wit, Snub stepped forward and presented the unhatched dragon egg to the massive beast as a peaceful offering; deeply respected by the gesture, the dragon claimed its offspring and presented Snub with a massive, magically resonant dragon scale before soaring back into the sky.

With the frontier cleared, the party rushed back to the obelisk of Wakenreth to reconvene with Dalamar. The elven mage welcomed you with a triumphant smile, revealing that his tireless translations of the ancient runes had successfully pinpointed the exact, hidden coordinates of the legendary flying city: the **City of Lost Names (Onyari)**.

The taskforce immediately mobilized the Kalaman army, marching across the final stretch of the wastes toward the coordinates. Along the way, the earth trembled as a *third* massive Purple Worm lunged from the dunes, but the party was now entirely practiced in giant-monster combat, rapidly slicing the beast to pieces without breaking stride.

At long last, you arrived at the colossal canyon hiding the ruins of the City of Lost Names—only to find your path completely blocked by the massive, heavily fortified main siege body of the Red Dragon Army. Suddenly, Darrett Highwater came riding through the dunes to meet you. To your immense delight, he was accompanied by a military transport bearing custom DHL packages from Kalaman: inside were your fully repaired, legendary **Dragonlance** and Ispin's signature **green shield**!

Recognizing that a frontal assault on the main host was suicide, Darrett coordinated with Cudgel to create a massive, roaring military diversion along the eastern ridge, drawing the bulk of the Dragon Army's forces away from the canyon floor. With the line temporarily broken, the party slipped through the trenches, located a hidden subterranean tunnel cutting through the rock face, and officially breached the perimeter of the lost city.
