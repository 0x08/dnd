# Session 18: The Path of Memories

**Date:** November 9, 1978

The party officially crossed the threshold into the ancient city ruins, entering a historical corridor known as the Path of Memories from a subterranean sector dubbed Wind’s End. The atmosphere inside was deeply oppressive; you found yourselves navigating a dark, winding stone tunnel echoing with the terrifying, hissing spiritual echoes of long-dead dragons. The supernatural dread took a heavy toll on the party's coordination, causing a sudden structural collapse that temporarily split the party into two separate groups.

Navigating the dark labyrinth, your progress was completely blocked by a massive, flooded stone passage. Utilizing the volatile orange crystals you had harvested from the Sunward Fortress, the party engineered an improvised demolition charge, successfully blowing the stone blockage to splinters and draining the chamber.

As you pressed forward through the rubble, the ancient defenses of the lost city activated. A towering, ancient Stone Golem roared to life from its pedestal, backed by a vicious, extraplanar Green Slaad that materialized from the shadows. A brutal, tactical skirmish ensued within the confined corridor, forcing the party to expend significant magical resources to shatter the golem and banish the chaotic frog-beast. Your trial was capped by a terrifying confrontation with a skeletal Death Dragon that lunged from a deep chasm, forcing the split party to fight with absolute desperation to collapse the undead horror back into the dust.
