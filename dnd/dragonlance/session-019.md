# Session 19: The Reforging of Legends

**Date:** January 25, 2026

Emerging from the dark tunnels into the majestic, ruined interior of the city, the party crossed paths with Demelin, an ancient, powerful elven archmage who had magically survived the passing of centuries. She sat with the group and shared the deep, tragic history of Onyari, explaining that it was originally a legendary flying city of pure pleasure built directly atop the conquered remains of dead dragons. She revealed that the historical Knight Sarlamir was originally dispatched to solve the city's corrupt magical imbalance, but his pride and ultimate disobedience during the Cataclysm had inadvertently made the curse infinitely worse. At the conclusion of her lecture, she presented the party with an updated copy of the *Rough Guide* to help navigate the ruins.

The party directed their steps toward the grand, crumbling Temple of Paladine. As you navigated the overgrown courtyard, your path was blocked by a massive, corrupted Treant that had been driven mad by the valley's lingering dark magic. The party executed a swift, coordinated skirmish, utilizing fire magic to pacify the wooden titan.

Entering the holy inner sanctum, you located the ancient, sacred forge of the temple. Utilizing your recovered spearhead and divine resources, the party conducted a holy ritual, successfully **reforging the legendary Dragonlance** to its absolute apex of divine power!

With the legendary weapon shining bright, the party embarked on a critical reconnaissance mission through the occupied sectors of the city. Spotting a heavy military supply line, you utilized your explosive resources to completely destroy a massive stone bridge, effectively crippling the Dragon Army's local logistics. Following up on the disruption, the party crept through the ruined streets, launching a stealth assault on a grand, occupied mansion that was currently serving as a local command outpost for the enemy leadership.
