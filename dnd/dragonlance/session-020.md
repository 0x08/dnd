# Session 20: The Threshold of the Heavens

**Date:** March 22, 2026

The party breached the grand perimeter doors, launching a total, surprise assault on the occupied mansion within the City of Lost Names. A fierce, bloody room-to-room urban battle erupted through the grand corridors. The party fought with absolute military precision, systematically cutting through multiple squads of disciplined Dragon Army soldiers and high-ranking Draconian officers. During a sweep of the ground floor, you located an ornately carved chest; after successfully bypassing a lethal needle trap woven into the lock, you looted a substantial horde of rare coins and valuable historical jewelry.

The party then advanced up the grand staircase to breach the upper master quarters. There you cornered and engaged the ruthless enemy commander, Hask. A fast and lethal boss battle ensued, but the party's overwhelming combat synergy rapidly brought the warlord crashing down. Thoroughly searching his corpse and private desk, you struck gold: you recovered a massive bundle of highly sensitive military dispatches and tactical maps, gaining invaluable intelligence regarding the movements of the Dragon Army's supreme leadership, specifically detailing the upcoming operations of Belephaion and Lozehet.

Armed with this definitive intelligence and with the legendary Dragonlance slung over your shoulder, the party exited the mansion and marched directly toward the grand, central focal point of the entire flying city: the awe-inspiring, magical monolith known as the **Threshold of the Heavens**.
