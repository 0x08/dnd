# Session 1

02/02/2025

## Bryn Shander

Vanera, Guldrak, Mael, Aero and Ash (me) arrived together in Bryn Shander, one of the ten northern towns, after a long and arduous trip. Very quickly they learnt a bit more about Auril, the Frostmaiden, who had tightened her icy grip over the northern lands. People often made sacrifices to Auril, to keep her pleased and to ensure that things would not get any worse.

Apparently there had been multiple murders recently in the area: a dwarf in Bryn Shander, a halfling in Targos and a human in Easthaven. Each of these murders had been committed with an ice shard as a weapon. Somebody called Sephek Kaltro was implicated, a man who apparently works as a bodyguard for somebody called Targas. Sephek apparently seemed very comfortable in the cold, so the fact that the murders were committed with ice shards would seem to link them to him.

The local tavern in Bryn is called "The North Look" and many strange encounters can be had there. The tavern features a knucklehead trout that is attached to the wall called Ol' Bitey. Outside the tavern the party was approached by three people (Ailsa, Olaf and ???) with a quest to retrieve a sled. The sled had been stolen by goblins and apparently contained ingots and somehow a group of yetis was also implicated.

## Bremen

The party then headed towards Bremen (why?) to follow up on some leads. Bremen famously lacks any real comfort, the only real place of interest being the local taverns (five of them), one of which is called the Grumpy Moose. Here the party learnt a bit more about the everlasting winter of Auril, an "evil" wizard caught in Easthaven and a monster which is said to live in the lake of Maer Dualdon.

The party followed further leads and ended up fighting goblins that had commandeered a sled pulled by polar bears. The goblins were killed and the bears escaped, but the party had to retreat when Sephek attacked and the group proved too weak for him. Afterwards the group returned to Bremen.

## Lake Maer Dualdon

After talking to the mayor, we went for a fishing trip on lake Maer Dualdon and heard even more rumours from somebody called Tali about a "monster" in the lake. We even caught some fish! We also met Grinsk, a fisher who promised to pay us for anything we catch.\*\*
