# 🧊 Session 2: Secrets of Ten-Towns

*02/03/2025*

## Lake Maer Dualdon

Whilst fishing on the lake, suddenly the lake started rumbling but after a short moment the rumbling stopped again. A small boat approached the party, a rock gnome called Zorin hailed the group as the boat got closer. Shortly afterwards another boat approached us carrying a flag of Targos, the boat then told us to stop fishing (but we didn't stop). Soon the water started rumbling again and a fully-fledged pleisiosaur appeared from under the surface and ate Aero! Zorin decided he liked us and asked whether he could join the party.

After heading back to shore, Grinsk paid us for the fish we caught and Tali was very curious about our encounter with the pleisiosaur.

## Bremen

The group headed back to Bremen to the Grumpy Moose, where Zorin gave some fish to the innkeeper to make some stew. After heading back to the campsite where we fought the goblins, we found some tracks that we were then able to follow and which lead us to Targos.

## Targos

Once we arrived in Targos, we headed to the local inn called the Luskan Arms, where innkeeper Owen keeps a tight ship. We spoke to town speaker Naerth who apparently had some flying snakes that could be sent out to warn about murdered Sephek.

Outside, a dog appeared which wanted us to follow it to a house, where a man called Kaegan then opened the door when we knocked. Kaegan told us that the dog was called Boy and that the dog belongs to a man named Gareth who led a party up Kelvin's Cairn about three days ago. I cast speak with animals on boy and the dog was able to tell me about avalanches that had occurred while climbing up the cairn and that Gareth had also been attacked. We decided to follow up on this lead and headed off towards Caer-Konig with boy as it is the place that is closest to the cairn.

## Caer-Konig

Once we arrived in Caer-Konig, we managed to acquire some climbing gear, which we definitely would need to climb up the cairn. Before setting off, we had a look around the village: it had an inn called the Northern Light and a tavern called the Hook, Line and Sinker.

## Kelvin's Cairn

With our climbing gear, we headed up the cairn until we found a few more dogs. These dogs were again able to tell us a bit more (via speak with animals) and we learnt that Gareth had been ambushed by a yeti. We were attacked by some crag cats, after dispersing those we headed up the mountain until we came to a cave with tracks of blood outside.
