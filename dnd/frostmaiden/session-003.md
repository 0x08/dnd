# 🏔️ Session 003: Blizzard Born

*22/06/2025*

## Kelvin's Cairn

After entering the cave, we managed to locate a yeti female and yeti infant inside. One of the party members called Fishfinger was also being kept in the cave. Once Fishfinger had been located, a large male yeti came back into the cave, but I managed to deter him from attacking us as I do speak yeti language. In exchange for leaving, we promised not to hurt the yeti female and infant.

After leaving the cave, we headed further up the cairn and found two more of the party frozen to death. We also managed to rescue Gareth (how??) and returned back to base camp and then to Caer-Konig.

## Caer-Konig

Back in town, we accompanied Gareth back to the Northern Light. We met Ally and Cory, who run the place. Apparently there had been some thefts recently, with goats, pearls and a magical lantern having gone missing. We quickly managed to recover the goats and the pearls by following tracks behind the inn, but could not find the lantern. More investigation seemed to suggest it might have been stolen and taken to a local duergar stronghold, so we set off towards it.

## Duergar Stronghold

Once we arrived at the stronghold, we slowly made our way inside and encountered quite a bit of resistance from angry duergar dwarves.
