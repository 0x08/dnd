# 🐺 Session 004: Chardalyn Shadows

*24/08/2025*

## Duergar Stronghold

The party cleared out the duergar outpost which was led by Nildar Sunblight, a duergar thief. Mael became the proud owner of a bucket while the others found the goods that were stolen from Caer-Konig, 2 goats, some beads and a magical colour changing lantern.

You also found a note from a certain Durth, who turned out to be Nildar's brother, in which they mentioned collecting chardalyn.

Chardalyn turned out to be a strong, crystal-like material which becomes as malleable as clay when treated with the proper tools and knowledge.

## Easthaven

Durth mentioned he had set up camp on the Easthaven ferry. (The lake has been frozen shut for weeks so the ferry is no longer in operation.)

Afterwards the party headed towards Easthaven because you had heard a wizard would be burned at the stake. All of you watched the poor man being roasted alive and while doing so, you were approached by Captain Arlaggath, the chief of the town's militia, who asked you to lead the search for 4 lost fishermen. When you were about to discuss the reward, Mael told the captain about Durth's note and town speaker Waylen (from Easthaven) was informed at once. He asked you to clear the ferry of any bandits that were present. You were given a kill licence.
