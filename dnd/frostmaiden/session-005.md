# 🏰 Session 005: Sunblight's Vengeance

*11/10/2025*

## Easthaven ferry

Ash shapeshifted into a horse, after which the horsing around promptly stopped and Durth Sunblight received the beating of a lifetime: Mael unleashed a thunderwave, Ash kicked away and, to add insult to injury, Vanera ferociously intimidated him after which he spilled the beans and told the party that his father, Xardorok Sunblight, has a secret castle in the mountains in which he is creating a chardalyn dragon with the intent of destroying all of Ten Towns.

Mael then decided to violate about 80% of the Geneva Convention by submitting Durth to the harshest prison regime imaginable, including being ball gagged and being hosed down twice on a daily basis.

## Cauldron caves

Captain Arlaggath gently urged you to go looking for the four missing fishermen which led you to a series of encounters in the cauldron caves on Lac Dinneshere: you encountered a water weird, an ice giant skeleton and Maud Chiselbone herself, a sea hag. You overpowered Maud who left you her cauldron of plenty (a magical object which feed a small village). For a change, you decided not to spare Maud's life as Mael stabbed her through the throat.
