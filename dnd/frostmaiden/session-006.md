# 🐉 Session 006: Destruction's Light

*16/11/2025*

## Easthaven

After having dispatched with Maud and her crazy soup cauldron, we headed back to Easthaven. Before heading back to the captain, we headed to the White Lady Inn to have some rest. The owner was a bard named Rinaldo, who apparently also organises seances to speak with “The Lady of the Lake. We obviously wanted to take part in such a seance, during which we learnt that there is evil in the lake, but also that evil cultists had taken over a castle in Caer-Dineval. After the seance, we managed to get a good night’s rest.

The next day we headed to captain Arlaggath with the cauldron, and we informed him that we had not been able to locate any survivors in the cave. As a reward he gave us a bag of tricks! The captain suggested that the town speaker might be interested in purchasing the cauldron from us.

Town speaker Waylen wanted the cauldron and offered us one gemstone worth 500GP each for it. At that moment he unfortunately only had two gemstones but he told us to come back in 48 hours for the rest; we left the cauldron with him and then headed off to find the cultists.

## Caer-Dineval

The village had both a tavern and an inn, however the inn (Diney’s Rest) had been closed for a very long time. We headed up the hill towards the tavern called The Uphill Climb.

Inside we found the owner, a man called Roark. He was a bit reluctant to talk to us at first, but after pushing him a bit we heard that the inn had not been doing very well at all lately. Apparently people in black robes had been coming and going to the castle.

Ash decides to check out the castle a bit first to get a better idea of what is going on. She shape shifts into a mouse and scurries around and gets a general idea of the layout, along with an idea of how many cultists there are. Once back, the party comes up with a ploy to capture a couple of cultists and interrogate them to get some more information. From that we learnt that the cultists are a group that call themselves the Knights of the Black Sword, led by somebody called Levistus.

The party then took the captured cultists' clothes to disguise themselves, Apparently the cultists also wear some kind of Amulet of the Black Sword which Vanera tried on but it made them feel rather strange.

Zorin and Ash made Guldrak and Mael invisible. Zorin and Vanera then disguised themselves whilst Ash shapeshifted into a horse that Zorin could ride. The plot worked and we made it into the castle! Once inside we then managed to kill about 12 of the cultists. Next up: Levistus!
