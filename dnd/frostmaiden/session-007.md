# 🐋 Session 007: Voyage of the Angajuk

*10/01/2026*

Zorin ended the previous battle by cutting off one the cultist’s pinky ‘out of curiosity’.

You went inside of the castle and met Mere who was a mere servant and not the sharpest tool in the shed. He told you the lay-out of the castle after which you decided to give a knock on Kadroth’s door. Kadroth knocked back but was quickly overpowered with the help of a magic boar. He turned out to be a bully towards Mere and he was also the leader of the Black Swords Cult. You offered Mere a chance at revenge in which he burned Kadroth with a glowing red poker. You then took Kadroth to the town speaker’s magnificent bedroom and he had no interest in Kadroth, he just wanted the cultist’s gone out of his castle as soon as possible.

Kadroth was then executed by Mere with the mere stab of a dagger but not before Kadroth had offered you all lifetime memberships of the Black Swords cult in the form of small chardalyn amulets. Speaker Crannock Siever was happy that the cultists were gone and told you that the party was always welcome. You also arranged for Mere to be permanently employed at the castle. Upstairs you admired the beautifully decorated bedroom of the town speaker and you also met Touché, Kadroth’s cat. She gave you some more info about the castle, its inhabitants and Kadroth’s behaviour.

You also encountered Hethyl, the cult’s soothsayer who had foreseen your arrival and knew her end was near. She shared information with you on Xardorok’s plans, namely that he's about to unleash a terrible doom upon Ten-Towns since he wants to become the first above-ground duergar ruler. She warned you not to face Xardorok too soon but to become even more powerful by exploring the outreaches of Icewind Dale. She also informed you that Xardorok’s forge is powered by the beating heart of a red dragon. She told you that the Black Swords hate the duergar and at the end of her message she died peacefully (with the help of Mael who had been opening all of the windows).

You then went on to explore the subterranean level of the castle, mainly because of rumours of delicious hams that were stored there and also because of some iced popsicles. In the catacombs you encountered Avarice who was part of the Blacks Swords Cult yet seemed to keep her distance from Kadroth and she also seemed to be pursuing other interests which she refused to share with you. You struck a deal with her and allowed her to keep her living quarters underneath the castle and she agreed to help you when needed in defeating the duergar and thwarting their plans.

You all became level 4 characters once you came out of the catacombs.
