# Session 8

*29/03/2026*

As level 4 characters you noticed that people are way more talkative and you were able to eavesdrop on some interesting stories:

- Angajuk’s Bell quest (whale with boat on back)
- A comet falling from the sky about a week ago, people are having difficulty sleeping because of a buzz they are hearing
- Goblin scavengers prowl on the tundra
- A secret buried tower that was connected to Dzaan, the wizard who was burned at the stake
- Two feuding goliath clans at the Spine of the World

After leaving Caer Dineval you went back to Easthaven for another séance with Rinaldo and the white lady of the lake. Rinaldo was happy to see you back and shared some info on what was going on in the Ten Towns region. The lady of the lake didn’t share that much information with you and you decided to head to Termalaine to clear out a mine which had been overrun by kobolds.

The mine proved less eventful than expected: most of the kobolds in the mine wanted to be left alone and the one who wanted to fight turned out to be possessed by a ghost: the ghost of a deceased botanist who wanted to complete his life’s work. Zorin snatched his satchel, which was then stolen from Ash by Termalaine’s town speaker who hasn’t been seen since. Mael discovered a psi crystal after knocking down a weird skull in the mine and has started hearing some kind of repetitive signal sent out by some kind of beacon.

At the end you decided to wait for Sephek and Torga. When they returned with the dog sled you ambushed them. Sephek wasn’t easy to overcome yet you ended up victorious. You were planning on bringing Sephek’s body to Bryn Shander.
