# Session 9

*25/05/2026*

## Bryn Shander

We headed to Bryn Shander with Sephek Kaltro's body. We meet Lynn Trollbane at the inn with the body. She rewards us with gold, silver and gemstones.

Some people approach us who have been suffering from a ringing noise in their heads for more than a week. Nothing obvious in common between them. Mael can sense the direction of the source, somewhere in the south towards the **Spine of the World**.

## Spine of the World

As we get closer we encounter two large worms, which Ash convinces to not attack us. The worms then escort us to the "ship", which is some kind of monstrosity which has crashed there. We enter the ship where there are chains and manacles designed to lock up creatures. There are four creatures roaming around inside the ship, each a bit stranger than the next.

We head upstairs to see somebody called Voryn, a sort of squid creature. He is happy that the "beacon" is being picked up and that it has led us to him. He's been broadcasting it for more than a week as an SOS. Apparently a psi-crystal would help fix the ship, Mael gave him the crystal that we found in the mine previously. We hang around a bit while he installs it to fix the "warp core". He gives us some kind of gun, which Mael takes. We find another gun in a crate, which Mael also takes. Voryn finds a pacifier rod.

After a while, Voryn comes back and tells us that he was able to fix the ship and they can leave again. They take off and head back into outer space.

As we head off, we see a monster burst out of the snow and head towards us. We fight! Ash summons a Giant Elk which stays with us for the rest of the day. The bulette is defeated.

## Tundra

We head towards the location where goblins have been reported to have been raiding from the tundra. As we get closer we notice trails and footprints, which we follow. We encounter two watch towers and Mael uses one of the weapons from Voryn on the tower, a lot of screaming erupts and then the sound of a horn being blown. Fight!

We kill the first four goblins and then we get the elk to ram the tower. Ash casts thunder wave on one of the towers and causes it to partly collapse. We kill all the goblins and next time we intend to enter the fortress.
