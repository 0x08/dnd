# Phandalin Campaign Dossier: The Lost Mine of Phandelver

## Notable Persons & Factions

- **Gundren Rockseeker:** A hopeful dwarven entrepreneur who, along with his brothers, discovered the long-lost entrance to Wave Echo Cave. His kidnapping serves as the anchor for the party's contract.
- **Sildar Hallwinter:** A distinguished member of the Lords' Alliance who was ambushed alongside Gundren. After his rescue, he establishes a temporary headquarters in Phandalin to investigate local disturbances.
- **The Redbrands:** A ruthless, mercenary bandit gang wearing distinctive scarlet cloaks, who have spent months terrorizing Phandalin from their hidden headquarters beneath Tresendar Manor.
- **Iarno "Glassstaff" Albrek:** A former wizard of the Lords' Alliance who went rogue, adopting the moniker "Glassstaff" due to his signature weapon. He took command of the Redbrands under orders from a higher master.
- **The Black Spider (Nezznar):** A mysterious, calculating villain operating from the shadows of the valley, utilizing an army of goblins, dopplegängers, and bugbears to seize control of Wave Echo Cave.

## Lost Relics & Locations

- **Wave Echo Cave:** A legendary subterranean cavern housing the ancient Forge of Spells, a mystical site capable of imbuing mundane weapons with permanent magical properties.
- **Thundertree:** A ruined, ash-choked village situated near the borders of Neverwinter Wood, currently overrun by toxic plant monsters, zombies, and a territorial young green dragon.
- **Cragmaw Castle:** A ruined, multi-towered stone stronghold hidden deep within the brush of Neverwinter Wood, serving as the throne room for the goblinoid chieftain King Grol.

## The Black Spider's Decrees

During your journey across the Triboar Trail, the Black Spider projected a magical psychic ultimatum to the party:

> *"So, finally we meet. For the first time and unfortunately for you this will also be the very last. You must be the gang of outlaws who is trying to stop me from claiming my rightful ownership of Wave Echo Cave. I can tell you that your efforts will be absolutely worthless: nothing will stop me from taking over Phandalin and the riches that the mine can provide. Thanks to your 'friend' Gundren I now have been able to locate the entrance of Wave Echo Cave and will soon be heading into the mine to claim what is rightfully mine. My minion Glasstaff was supposed to put a halt to your efforts in the Redbrand hideout but he fled like a coward... now he has the opportunity to redeem himself by sending me your heads after this encounter. I bid you farewell... you have been most unworthy opponents."*
