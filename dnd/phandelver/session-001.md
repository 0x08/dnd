# Session 1: Ambush on the Triboar Trail

The campaign began with a frantic clash of steel along the high road. While escorting a supply wagon down the trail toward the frontier settlement of Phandalin, the party was suddenly ambushed by a coordinated raiding party of Cragmaw goblins. Springing into a defensive formation, you fought back fiercely, driving your blades through three of the raiders while successfully pinning and capturing a lone goblin named Grom.

With the immediate threat neutralized, you secured the captured goblin, gathered the scattered reins, and successfully escorted the supply cart into Phandalin. Your first stop was Barthen's Provisions, where you officially delivered the much-needed crates and supplies to the grateful storekeeper. Seeking a secure place to base your operations, you booked rooms at the local Stonehill Inn and dragged the bound Grom down into the town's makeshift dungeon cells for overnight confinement.

The following morning, the party mobilized and traveled back to the original ambush site to search for tracks. Using a combination of stern threats and promises of survival, you coerced Grom into acting as a scout. The terrified goblin capitulated, leading the party through a series of hidden wilderness trails directly to the camouflaged mouth of the notorious Cragmaw Hideout.
