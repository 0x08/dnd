# Session 3: The Rumors of Phandalin

The party made a triumphant return to Phandalin, booking comfortable rooms at the Stonehill Inn to rest and gather local intelligence. Over flagons of ale, the tavern patrons shared a wealth of troubling rumors regarding the region, detailing the suspicious activities of Sister Garaele at the local shrine, a rising threat of Orc Raiders along the frontier, and the brutal murder of a local woodcarver named Thel Dendrar. You also spoke with a young boy named Pip, who shared a secret about a hidden path in the woods, and learned of the Lionshield Coster merchant cartel.

The next morning, you visited the Lionshield Coster exchange building, presenting the merchant mistress with the recovered Blue Lion crates and receiving a handsome gold reward for your honesty. From there, you paid a visit to the Townmaster’s hall to review the local bounties. You found that Sildar Hallwinter had officially established a temporary command office within the building, actively launching an investigation into the mysterious disappearance of a prominent Lords' Alliance wizard named Iarno Albrek.

Your next stop was the town shrine, where Sister Garaele officially contracted the party to locate the legendary banshee Agatha, presenting you with a specific magical quest to extract crucial information from the spirit. Seeking to test the local opposition, the party marched down to the rowdy Sleeping Giant taproom, a notorious hangout for the scarlet-cloaked bandit gang known as the Redbrands. A brief, violent barroom brawl erupted. You successfully knocked out three Redbrand ruffians, dragging the final surviving thug into an alley until he terrifiedly spilled his secrets.

The captive revealed that the gang operated under the absolute command of a mysterious, staff-wielding wizard known as "Glassstaff." He admitted that the murdered woodcarver Thel Dendrar’s body had been dragged down to their lair, and his grieving wife and children had been locked away in iron cages as slaves. Armed with the precise location of the hideout, the party marched out to the perimeter of Tresendar Manor, utilizing Pip's secret woodland path to discover a dark, overgrown entrance leading directly into the underdark tunnels.
