# Session 6: The Orkish Masquerade

The party kicked off the morning by purchasing a sturdy supply cart and a pair of horses from the Phandalin stables. You loaded your gear and traveled northeast along the high Triboar Trail. Along the road, you intercepted a goblin scouting party; after a brief skirmish, you successfully extracted the precise, hidden location of Cragmaw Castle, learning it sat nestled deep within the southern edge of the dense Neverwinter Wood.

Continuing along the trail, you arrived at the haunted, overgrown clearing of Conyberry and invoked the ritual to summon Agatha the Banshee. As the terrifying spirit materialized from the mist, Gillian stepped forward and confidently used the party's singular question to ask: *"Am I the most beautiful of them all?"* Flattered or amused by the sheer audacity of the bard, the spirit provided her answers before vanishing back into the ether. The party then established a secure camp within the ruins of Conyberry to secure a long rest.

The next morning, you steered the cart south toward the rocky bluffs of Wyvern Tor to hunt down a disruptive band of Orc Raiders. Upon locating the dark mouth of their mountain cave, the party chose to execute a brilliant, high-stakes tactical deception rather than a frontal assault. Gillian utilized his magic to disguise himself as a hulking orc warrior, while Ulvor dirtied his armor to follow closely behind acting as Gillian's miserable "servant." Approaching the lone orc sentry guarding the threshold, Gillian delivered an incredibly convincing vocal performance, completely fooling the guard and commanding the gullible orc to turn around and happily lead them directly inside the main chieftain's cavern.
