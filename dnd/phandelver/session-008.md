# Session 8: The Ruined Village of Thundertree

After restocking your packs with fresh rations and adventuring gear in Phandalin, the party traveled north to the great coastal city of Neverwinter. You spent the evening resting inside a serene local monastery, reuniting with various friends and family members before heading down to a local tavern to get thoroughly drunk. The following morning, you shook off the alcohol and marched east along the river basin toward the ash-choked ruins of Thundertree.

Upon crossing the threshold of the overgrown village, you were met by a weathered druid named Reidoth. He delivered a stern warning regarding the local landscape, noting that the ruins were currently crawling with hostile twig blights, giant spiders, and irradiated ash zombies. He passionately requested the party's assistance in driving off a dangerous young green dragon that had recently claimed the town's central stone tower as its personal roost.

The party systematically swept through the ruined cottages, clearing out pockets of monsters. While searching the collapsed remains of a wealthy manor, you successfully uncovered the lost emerald heirloom necklace that Mirna Dendrar had told you about. The exploration took a terrifying turn when a massive Giant Spider lunged from the rafters to attack Kevin; the sheer horror of the arachnid's bite caused Kevin to suffer a severe emotional breakdown in the middle of the combat. After stabilizing him, you pushed into a fortified structure and discovered a coven of dark dragon cultists. Driven by sudden fury, Kevin launched an immediate, unprovoked assault on their lines, sending the cultists screaming out into the open streets as a frantic skirmish began.
