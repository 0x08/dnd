# Session 10: The Fall of King Grol

The confrontation inside the stone tower was an absolute crucible of fire and poison. Venomfang reared back and unleashed a devastating torrent of toxic poison breath across the chamber, instantly knocking Kevin unconscious and leaving him bleeding out on the floor. The party rallied with desperate heroism; while the frontline fighters traded brutal blows with the green dragon, the spellcasters administered clutch healing magic to stabilize Kevin. Through an incredible display of martial violence, you broke the dragon's resolve, dealing a fatal blow that sent Venomfang crashing down dead amidst his hoard.

A profoundly grateful Reidoth thanked the party for saving the region from the draconic shadow, fulfilling his bargain by guiding the taskforce through a series of secret forest trails directly to the gates of Cragmaw Castle. After bidding the druid farewell, the party approached the brooding stone fortress. Creeping up to a reinforced side door, you jammed a crowbar into the seam and used brute physical force to pop the iron hinges.

The party flooded into the castle corridors, launching a swift assault that cut through a defensive line of surprised Hobgoblins and goblin sentries. You methodically breached the central chambers, throwing open the doors to the high throne room to find a chaotic scene: the massive bugbear King Grol was in the middle of a tense negotiation with a disguised dopplegänger shapeshifter, while the unconscious, battered body of Gundren Rockseeker lay draped across the floorboards. A fast, brutal melee erupted. The party cut down King Grol and executed the shapeshifter before it could escape. Ulvor rushed to the center of the room, uncorking a potent healing potion and pouring it down Gundren's throat, successfully reviving the dwarven merchant as he leaned shakily against the castle wall surrounded by the corpses of his captors.
