# Session 11: The Map to Wave Echo Cave

With Gundren Rockseeker safely breathing and resting against the throne room wall, the party set out to systematically clear the remaining wings of Cragmaw Castle. While exploring a reinforced northern tower, you accidentally breached a high-security cell and were instantly attacked by a roaring, ferocious Owlbear that had been locked away by the goblins; the party formed a tight defensive line and successfully put the monster down. Returning to the empty throne room, you conducted a thorough search of King Grol's filthy bedframe, successfully locating Gundren's highly coveted, handwritten map hidden deep inside the mattress padding.

Disaster struck as the party retraced their steps through the grand main hall. Wilbin misstepped and accidentally triggered a hidden mechanical pressure plate, setting off a loud, clanging alarm trap that echoed through the entire fortress structure. The trap instantly alerted the remaining garrison; dozens of armed Goblins and disciplined Hobgoblins came streaming out of the barracks like a tidal wave to surround the party. A fierce, bottleneck battle erupted in the center of the hall, but the party held their ground, painting the stone floors crimson until the entire goblinoid force was completely eradicated.

With Cragmaw Castle officially cleared of all evil, the party organized a secure litter to transport Gundren. You traveled back to the coast to retrieve your supply cart from Neverwinter before completing the long journey back to Phandalin. Arriving back at the Townmaster’s hall, you reunited Gundren with a deeply relieved Sildar Hallwinter. With the map recovered and the dwarf brothers reunited, both Gundren and Sildar expressed an immense, burning eagerness to immediately launch an expedition into the depths of Wave Echo Cave.
