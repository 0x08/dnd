# Session 12: Infiltration of the Mine

The morning began with a grand celebration at the Townmaster's hall. True to his word, a weeping Gundren Rockseeker formally rewarded the party for his salvation, signing an official decree promising the taskforce a permanent 10% share of all the mine's wealth the moment the excavation operation was up and running. Before departing, the party paid a visit to Sister Garaele’s shrine, purchasing a fresh crate of potent magical potions for the dungeon crawl ahead. Later that afternoon, Gillian took center stage at the Stonehill Inn, loudly singing grand epic songs about his own beauty and heroism while the rest of the crew drank to success.

Following Gundren's map, the party traveled into the jagged mountains and located the dark, yawning mouth of Wave Echo Cave. Lighting your torches, you stepped into the ancient subterranean tunnels, immediately noticing a rhythmic, booming sound like crashing ocean waves echoing through the stone every few minutes.

The initial descent proved incredibly treacherous as the ancient security systems of the mine activated. The party was first ambushed by a slow-moving colony of corrosive Ochre Jellies that dropped from the ceiling, but you split your attacks effectively to dissolve them. Pushing deeper into the dark tunnels, you were swarmed by a pack of feral, flesh-eating Ghouls that lunged from the shadows of an old mining tracks; the frontline fighters formed a defensive wall and cut them down. Finally, as you crossed a wide cavern chasm, a massive cloud of blood-drinking Stirges swarmed out of the stalactites, but the party deployed quick area-of-effect magic to swat the monsters from the air and clear the passage.
