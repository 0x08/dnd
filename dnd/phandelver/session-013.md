# Session 13: The Forge of Spells

The party advanced deeper into the subterranean maze of Wave Echo Cave, with the mysterious, booming thunder of the cavern echoing through your chest cavity every few minutes. While exploring a southern junction, the group was ambushed by a squad of ancient skeleton archers; you rapidly closed the distance and smashed the bones to dust. Seeking to investigate a patch of strange vegetation, Wilbin casually poked a cluster of glowing mushrooms, inadvertently unleashing a cloud of poisonous spores that left him coughing and sputtering.

Turning a corner into a grand, reinforced laboratory, the party came face-to-face with an ancient guardian: a multi-eyed Spectator floating over a glowing green hearth. The aberration declared it was under eternal orders to guard the legendary Forge of Spells. Utilizing exceptional diplomacy and quick-witted deception, the party successfully convinced the extraplanar guardian that the ancient war was over and all threats had passed; satisfied that its contract was complete, the Spectator blinked its multiple eyes and peacefully de-manifested back to its home plane. Searching the glowing Brazier of Green Flame, the party uncovered two legendary magical artifacts: the glowing mace *Lightbringer* and the magnificent steel breastplate *Dragonguard*.

Your celebration was instantly cut short as a horde of shuffling zombies came spilling out of the northern tunnels, led by a terrifying, floating green skull wreathed in fire—a Flameskull. A chaotic firefight erupted. In a bizarre turn of events, the volatile Flameskull attempted to cast a high-level *fireball* spell within the cramped stone corridor, inadvertently caught in its own blast radius and committing spectacular accidental suicide. With the area clear, Kaz sent his faithful falcon, Bloodwing, soaring across the expanse of the Great Cavern to map out the terrain ahead.
