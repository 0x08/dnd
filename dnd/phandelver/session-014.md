# Session 14: The Great Cavern

Following Bloodwing's aerial scouting report, the party advanced into the massive, echoing expanse of the Great Cavern. The quiet was shattered as a pack of feral ghouls ambushed your flank, but you swiftly put them down with practiced steel. Moving toward a set of reinforced iron doors, the frontline fighters stacked up and violently forced the threshold open, completely catching a large squad of Bugbear occupiers off-guard and slaughtering them before they could form a defensive line.

Pushing deeper into the northern tunnels, you discovered a breathtaking subterranean lake filling a massive cavern bowl. As you mapped the shoreline, you spotted an advanced troop of Bugbears aggressively searching the rocky crevices for hidden treasure. Seizing the tactical initiative, Ulvor fearlessly leaped straight down into the rocky pit below, instantly finding himself completely surrounded by the furry brutes. A chaotic, high-stakes melee followed; despite the terrible positioning, the party provided heavy ranged support from the ledges, allowing Ulvor to paint the stones red and eventually slaughter every single bugbear in the pit.

With the immediate sector cleared of hostiles, a hilarious wave of competitive energy washed over the group. To break the tension of the dungeon crawl, Gillian and Wilbin marched over to a deep cavern chasm and engaged in a highly animated, competitive pissing competition to see who could clear the gap. Amused but practical, Alex stepped forward with his divine resources, systematically administering healing magic to patch up every single battered party member—purposefully skipping Gillian entirely to teach the bard a lesson about vanity.
