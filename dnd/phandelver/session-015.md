# Session 15: Showdown with the Black Spider

The final expedition through the deep recesses of Wave Echo Cave yielded an incredible initial find: tucked away within a forgotten iron lockbox, the party discovered and claimed a pair of magnificent **Gauntlets of Ogre Strength**.

Weapon in hand, you breached the grand double doors leading into the magnificent central temple of the mine—and finally came face-to-face with the Nezznar, the Black Spider. The dross villain stood atop a high stone dais, flanked by a massive army of heavily armed bugbears and a trio of skittering giant spiders. Holding Gundren’s sobbing, captive brother by the hair, the Black Spider delivered a final, villainous monologue, mocking Glassstaff’s failure in Phandalin and demanding that the party immediately drop their weapons and surrender the Forge of Spells, or watch the dwarf brother die.

The party responded with a unified battle cry, launching an absolute, no-compromises final assault. The temple chamber erupted into a spectacular storm of steel, fire, and divine light. While the frontline fighters engaged the giant spiders and formed a defensive wall against the charging bugbears, the spellcasters unleashed their ultimate arcane payloads directly at the dais. Through sheer tactical brilliance and uncompromising violence, the party shattered the enemy lines, cutting down the bugbear army and permanently ending the Black Spider's reign of terror. Defeated and victorious, the party stood triumphant in the reclaimed halls of Wave Echo Cave, securing the future of Phandalin forever!
