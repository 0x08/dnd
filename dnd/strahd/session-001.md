# Session 1: The Invitation and the Mists

Following your recent exploits, the party settled down for a period of rest in the city of Neverwinter. During this time, Gundren Rockseeker and his brother were incredibly active after being rescued from the clutches of the Black Spider. The brothers successfully reopened the Wave Echo Cave mine, and business flourished as they eagerly auctioned off magical items to the highest bidders along the Sword Coast. By regularly escorting these high-value magical transports, you crossed paths with many prominent figures. Among them was Duchess Morwen of Daggerford, an avid collector of the arcane who frequently purchased the brothers' wares. Over time, you developed a close friendship with her, which ultimately resulted in an exclusive invitation to her estate.

While attending Duchess Morwen’s grand banquet, the hostess requested a favor: she asked the party to rid the town of a band of mysterious travelers who had set up camp just outside the castle walls. When you went to confront them, the travelers shared captivating stories of a noble prince and a terrible curse they desperately wished to lift. Moved by their plight, you agreed to accompany them to meet Madam Eva, who they claimed could explain everything.

The party set off toward the south inside the Vistani wagons. After a couple of days of uneventful travel, a thick, suffocating fog suddenly rolled in, swallowing the road. When the mists finally parted, you found yourselves enclosed within a dark, oppressive forest, eventually arriving at a set of towering iron gates that swung open and closed entirely on their own. That night, after establishing camp, Wilbin suffered through a series of terrifying experiences and a vivid fever dream, catching a glimpse of a dark figure looking down from a brooding castle at a party of newly arrived adventurers.

The next day, you pressed forward through the gloomy town of Barovia without stopping, passing by a set of deeply unsettling gallows before finally arriving at the Vistani camp nestled next to Tser Pool. After spending the evening singing and dancing with the welcoming Vistani, Madam Eva approached the group and offered to read your fortunes. You accepted, and she revealed five mystical Tarokka cards that laid out the perilous path ahead.
