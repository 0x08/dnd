# Session 6: Fire and Bones

The upper floor of the coffin maker's shop erupted into chaos as the vampire spawn attacked. A brutal, destructive battle followed, during which the party unleashed a devastating barrage of fire and lightning magic. While the onslaught successfully turned the tide and eradicated the vampire spawn, the sheer arcane force destroyed half of the wooden building in a roaring inferno.

Amid the smoking rubble of the upstairs cupboards, you successfully recovered the precious bones of St. Andral along with a small pouch of hidden coins. The party rushed back to the church and returned the relics to a profoundly grateful Father Lucian, ensuring the spiritual wards over the sanctuary were restored. With the immediate crisis averted, you headed back to the safety of the Blue Water Inn to reconvene, rest, and discuss your next moves with Ireena, Ismark, and the children.
