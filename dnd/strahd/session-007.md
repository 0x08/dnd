# Session 7: The Road to Berez and Argynvostholt

The party reunited with Ireena, Ismark, and the kids at the Blue Water Inn, finding that they had successfully organized comfortable lodgings for everyone upstairs. During your stay, you were introduced to the Martikov family: Urwin, Danika, and their young boys, Brom and Bay. After spending a restful night at the inn, you departed the following morning, making a stop at the church to leave Ireena and Ismark under the protective supervision of Father Lucian.

Free to travel unburdened, the party headed down the highway toward Krezk. Along the way, you encountered a ghostly skeleton knight riding a phantom warhorse; intrigued, you followed the apparition until it crumbled into dust, leaving a trail that pointed down the road toward the ruins of Berez. Following the lead, you arrived in the abandoned settlement of Berez, crossed the rushing river, and met a mysterious scout named Muriel. She warned you of the horrific witch Baba Lysaga, who resided deep within the Berez swamp inside a bizarre, creeping hut. Muriel also shared her firm belief that Baba Lysaga was operating in league with Strahd. Weighing the danger, you decided to postpone a confrontation, turn back toward Krezk, and stick to your original travel plan.

On the return journey, you opted to make a detour to inspect the grand Argynvostholt mansion. Arriving at the ruined estate, you were greeted by a massive, weathered statue of a silver dragon sitting prominently outside. However, the moment you stepped forward to cross the threshold and enter the mansion, the stone dragon's mouth suddenly gaped open, unleashing a long, freezing gust of ice-cold air across the party.
