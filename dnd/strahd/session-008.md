# Session 8: The Haunted Manor

Bracing against the supernatural chill, the party pushed past the dragon statue and entered the mansion, moving through the grand main foyer and into the dark dining room. Inside, your attention was drawn to an ornate chandelier that cast a bright, warm illumination despite having absolutely no candles lit; Wilbin investigated the anomaly and determined that a permanent magical spell had been woven into the fixture.

Leaving the dining room behind, you cautiously entered the manor's desecrated chapel. There, you were ambushed by three vengeful undead knights, but through swift steel and divine power, you successfully laid the spirits to rest. With the immediate threats neutralized, you doubled back to systematically map the remainder of the ground floor. Your exploration led you straight into a dark chamber housing a massive horde of giant spiders, though the party coordinated seamlessly and easily dispatched the arachnids.
