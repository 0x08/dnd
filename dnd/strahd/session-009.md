# Session 9: The Holy Symbol and the Dragon's Wrath

The morning began by clearing out the few remaining giant spiders nesting in the corners of the room. As you continued your search of the manor, you encountered the restless spirit of a dragon swirling within a large fireplace. Pressing deeper into the estate's bizarre interior, you discovered a gruesome sight: a severed head that bore an exact, terrifying resemblance to Gillian. Kevin immediately cast *detect magic* on the gruesome object, revealing that it was merely a cruel and sophisticated illusion. Shortly after, the party found a stray bat nesting inside an old ceramic pot that strangely seemed convinced Wilbin was its mother, followed by a tiny dragonet that burst from a hearth and flew rapidly up into the higher levels of the manor.

Ascending the stairs, you crossed paths with the spectral spirits of Sir Godfrey and Vladimir Horngaard. A grim and bitter phantom, Vladimir gruffly suggested that you speak with the Lord Commander, eventually giving a begrudging promise that he would aid the party against Strahd when the final battle arrived. Continuing to the absolute apex of the manor's tower, you hit a major milestone: you discovered the Holy Symbol of Ravenkind.

The party paused to take a short, well-deserved rest, during which Kevin sat quietly and successfully attuned to the powerful holy relic. Eager to test its capabilities and share the triumph, Kevin suggested they show the artifact to Vladimir Horngaard. However, Vladimir was completely unamused when Kevin accidentally shone a blinding ray of pure sunlight straight into his spectral face. Perceiving the act as a hostile insult, the ancient knight drew his greatsword and furiously attacked the group.
