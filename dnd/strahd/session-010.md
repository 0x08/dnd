# Session 10: The Lightning Tower

The party managed to de-escalate the situation and narrowly avoid an all-out battle with the furious Vladimir Horngaard. Seizing the opportunity, you quickly evacuated the castle and hurried back onto the trail toward Krezk. As night fell, you established a secure campsite, sheltering comfortably under the magical protection of Gillian’s conjured hut while enjoying a feast of dream pies. During the quiet evening hours, Gillian experimented with his bagpipes and made a spectacular discovery: the instrument could be utilized effectively as both a sonic weapon and a musical outlet.

The next day, the party arrived at a major crossroads. Taking pride in public works, Wilbin took some time to repair a broken signpost, ensuring that future travelers could find their way through Barovia. From the intersection, you directed your steps north toward a lonely tower standing by the shores of Lake Baratok. Upon arrival, you noticed a colorful Vistani wagon parked outside. Kaz stepped forward and attempted to open the tower's heavy front door by turning the handle, inadvertently triggering a massive magical trap. Jagged arcs of lightning flashed violently up and down the length of the masonry, severely injuring Kaz, Gillian, and Kevin.

Remembering the cryptic clues you had gathered, Ulvor stepped up to the door and performed a precise, jiggly dance according to the ancient instructions, successfully unlocking the threshold. Inside the tower, the party solved a mechanical puzzle, figuring out how to command a set of stone statues to operate a central elevator platform. Riding it to the top floor, you discovered a furnished living space indicating that someone had recently lived here and meticulously tended to the grounds. You also uncovered a gruesome trophy hidden inside a chest: a severed Vistani head, which Ulvor casually claimed and packed away. However, your triumph was cut short when you peered out the high windows to find a massive, snarling pack of wolves and werewolves waiting for you on the ground below.
