# Session 11: Werewolves and Mountain Passes

Standing at the base of the tower, the fierce werewolf alpha Kiril stepped forward and loudly demanded that the party throw down their weapons and submit to a mandatory visit with Count Strahd. Completely ignoring the threat, the party launched a coordinated assault from the tower, initiating a bloody skirmish that resulted in the slaughter of nearly the entire pack. A single werewolf named Nala survived the onslaught; cornered, she shared vital intelligence regarding their pack dynamics, explaining that Kiril's leadership was currently being challenged by a rival named Emil. She also revealed the precise location of their hidden den located far to the west of the tower.

Following a brief rest to bandage your wounds, Ulvor approached the Vistani wagon parked outside and attempted to pry it open. The moment he pulled down the main external lever, the entire vehicle exploded in a brilliant flash of fire, knocking both Wilbin and Gillian completely unconscious. The party scrambled to administer healing magic and, once stabilized, began traveling south toward a landmark that Bloodwing had previously spotted from the air.

As you traveled along the mountain path, you were approached by a cloaked, remarkably camp servant of Strahd who politely delivered a formal, written invitation to dine safely at Castle Ravenloft. Pressing past the encounter, the road eventually led you to the treacherous Tsolenka Pass. Using your magical resources, you successfully dispelled a roaring wall of green flame blocking the path, allowing you to enter an ancient guard tower where you discovered nothing but old, dust-covered skeletons.
