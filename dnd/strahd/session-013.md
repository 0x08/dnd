# Session 13: The Vaults of Cold and Fire

Wilbin’s initial attempts to scale the smooth amber statue ended in disaster as he lost his footing and fell hard to the floor multiple times. Growing frustrated, he cast *spider climb*, allowing him to easily stroll up the masonry to inspect the eye holes before returning safely to the ground. The ambient temperature within the temple continued to plummet, forcing the party to make saving throws against extreme, supernatural cold. Wilbin succumbed to the chill, dropping to level 2 exhaustion, while Ulvor managed to fare slightly better, remaining at level 1 exhaustion.

As you crept through the quiet corridors, you spotted a group of wicked witches snooping around the doors and experimenting with various passwords. The party sprang into action, swiftly executing the witches and smashing their animated flying brooms to splinters. Afterward, you used your magical resources to dispel an *arcane lock* spell sealing one of the heavy doors.

Behind the threshold lay a grim chamber filled with ancient stone sarcophagi—and another trio of deadly flameskulls. A furious firefight erupted, and the hags' fiery magic nearly brought the party to the brink of death before you finally extinguished them. The toll of the temple was severely draining: Wilbin and Ulvor were now suffering from level 2 exhaustion, while Kevin and Gillian were weighed down by level 1 exhaustion. Seeking a silver lining, you explored an upper room and discovered a dormant Shield Guardian standing ready for a master. The grueling expedition paused as you halted your exploration at chamber X40.
