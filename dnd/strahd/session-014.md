# Session 14: Dark Gifts and Darker Desires

The party resumed their exploration and discovered a grand chamber overflowing with glittering treasure, though a closer inspection revealed a menacing, invisible statue standing guard over the hoard; wisely, you opted to leave the wealth completely untouched. Moving into an adjacent room, you were suddenly ambushed by four malevolent specters that telekinetically hurled broken junk and debris from the floorboards to batter the group. You laid the spirits to rest and thoroughly searched the room, uncovering a valuable *scroll of wall of fire* and a powerful *staff of frost*. Upon claiming the staff, Wilbin's mind was warped by its dark magic, causing him to gain a terrible new flaw: an insatiable, overwhelming crave for power above all else.

Deep within the temple's hidden recesses, you also discovered an incredibly detailed, miniature architectural model of Castle Ravenloft. Descending to the lower levels, the party crossed paths with a group of strange, babbling nothics and managed to engage them in conversation, extracting information about the layout of the temple. Following their guidance, you made your way toward the legendary Amber Vault. However, before you could claim its secrets, you had to fight for your lives against six bloodthirsty vampire spawn that were nesting in the dark, successfully defeating them after a fierce struggle.
