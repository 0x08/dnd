# Session 15: The Tome of Strahd

Inside the eerie silence of the Amber Vault, Wilbin approached and boldly touched one of the glowing amber sarcophagi. The dark vestige trapped within spoke directly into his mind, whispering that he must return only when he had grown far more powerful. Realizing the party was utterly spent, Gillian cast his magical hut right there within the vault, allowing everyone to enjoy a safe, undisturbed long rest.

The next morning, you ascended to the temple's ancient library. After a meticulous search of the dusty shelves, you skyrocketed your resources by uncovering your primary objective: the legendary **Tome of Strahd**, alongside several other rare books and arcane spellbooks. Thoroughly exploring the remaining wings of the complex, you navigated a series of hidden secret passages, discovering a strange V-shaped amulet hidden directly behind a blackboard in a grand auditorium. Your exploration was violently interrupted when a massive flesh golem blindsided the group, brutally thwomping Wilbin once again before you managed to tear it apart. Afterward, you cleared out a pack of foul ghasts lurking at the termination of a secret tunnel, before finally returning to the massive piles of treasure you had bypassed earlier to conduct some serious, well-deserved looting.
