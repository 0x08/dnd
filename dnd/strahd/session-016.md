# Session 16: Treasures of the Temple

With the temple largely cleared of immediate threats, the party focused on organizing their massive haul of treasure. You took time to fully activate and command your new Shield Guardian, whom you officially named Shaloth. Shortly after, you crossed paths with your old foxlike adversary; the party left no room for escape this time, trapping and swiftly killing the creature.

An inspection of the fox's remains yielded a pair of magical spectacles and a highly versatile *robe of useful items*, both of which were claimed by the party. To solve the logistical problem of transporting your massive haul of coins and relics, you packed the most valuable treasures into a sturdy chest and commanded the tireless Shaloth to carry it. With your powerful new Shield Guardian marching in lockstep, the party prepared to finally exit the freezing depths of the mountain temple.
