# Session 17: The Roc of Tsolenka Pass

The heavily laden party began their descent from the peaks, heading back down the snow-covered mountain road toward Tsolenka Pass. However, as you were cautiously making your way across the high, narrow bridge, a shadow fell over the chasm as a colossal, monstrous bird materialized from the clouds and swooped down to attack. The massive beast dove directly into the party's ranks, snapping its beak and talons before seizing Kevin in its massive claws and lifting him into the air.

Refusing to watch his ally be carried off, Ulvor made a daring leap, jumping clean off the bridge to land squarely onto the monstrous bird's back. A dramatic battle in mid-air ensued as the party unleashed ranged attacks from the bridge. Together, you managed to deal a fatal blow to the creature, but as it died, it dropped Kevin into the roaring river far below. Reacting instantly, Wilbin cast *feather fall* to slow Kevin's rapid descent, while Ulvor fearlessly jumped down into the canyon after him. After scrambling down the treacherous cliffs to the shoreline, the rest of the party reunited with Kevin and Ulvor by the water's edge, where you established a secure camp and took a much-needed long rest by the lakeside.
