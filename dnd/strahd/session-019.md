# Session 19: Clearing the Vineyards

The party immediately began interrogating the captured druid, demanding answers about their presence at the vineyard. She revealed that they had launched this assault as a counter-attack after being harassed by a mysterious league of "ravens." She further explained that her circle had stolen a priceless "treasure" from these ravens, who had subsequently been trying to steal it back. You bound and gagged the prisoner for later use, but she was unfortunately killed during a chaotic skirmish shortly thereafter.

The party then systematically cleared the entire winery building of remaining druids and blights. During your search of the facility, you discovered a functional printing press and a whimsical toy horse crafted by the legendary toymaker Blinsky. You also uncovered a detailed shipping manifest outlining exactly where the winery's shipments were traditionally delivered. Deep within the building, you encountered a particularly deranged druid wielding a twisted *Gulthias staff*; you subdued him, stripped him of the powerful artifact, and securely tied him up. Moving down into the dark wine cellar, you fought off another druid and a fresh wave of blights, discovering a hidden secret cold-room filled with brown mold that was being cleverly utilized as a natural refrigerator. With the facility secure, you headed back outside to speak with Davian, intending to ask him about the stolen treasure and warn him about a vat of wine you discovered had been poisoned.
