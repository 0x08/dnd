# Session 20: The Ritual of Yester Hill

Davian Martikov expressed immense gratitude to the party for liberating the winery, and he acknowledged the grim news that one of their primary vats of wine had indeed been severely poisoned. He shared the deep history of the winery, explaining how the estate had been passed down through the generations of the Martikov family. Davian then revealed the secret to their success: three magical gems that uniquely allowed the vineyards to grow in the harsh Barovian climate. Tragically, all three had been stolen. One gem remained completely unaccounted for, though Urwin back in Vallaki reportedly held more information. The second had likely been seized by the swamp witch Baba Lysaga, and the third had been taken by the dark druids of Yester Hill.

The party immediately marched southwest toward Yester Hill, sending Bloodwing soaring ahead to scout the terrain. Halfway to the destination, you paused to take a long rest to prepare for the coming conflict. Upon resuming the approach, Gillian and Wilbin utilized the dense brush to travel stealthily through the woods, while the rest of the party marched openly down the main road.

At the foot of the hill, you were shocked to encounter Strahd von Zarovich himself, who casually reiterated his formal invitation to dinner at his castle before vanishing. Pushing up the steep incline, Gillian used his magic to disguise himself, blending in and dancing among the frenzied druids to disrupt their ritual. Suddenly, a colossal, terrifying Tree Blight burst forth from a massive wooden statue on the hilltop. Kaz reacted instantly, shattering the captured *Gulthias staff* over his knee, which unleashed a magical feedback loop that successfully killed the massive plant monstrosity. However, the battlefield remained highly volatile as a large force of wild Berserkers and corrupted Druids swarmed in to surround the party.
