# Session 22: The Gates of Krezk

The party journeyed back toward Krezk with the wine wagon in tow. As you approached the village bridge, you stumbled upon a horrific scene: a body that had been brutally ripped to pieces, which turned out to be the tragic remains of a young child. Showing deep respect, you placed the small child's remains into the stone sarcophagus you had transported all the way from the Amber Temple, burying them properly to the somber, echoing tunes of Gillian's bagpipes.

You pressed onward to the gates of Krezk, hailing the guards and speaking once more with Burgomaster Krezkov. True to his word, he opened the gates and welcomed you inside, explaining that Krezk was a strictly self-sufficient village harboring no inns or taverns. He shared details about the imposing Abbey of Saint Markovia overlooking the town, the mysterious Abbot who ruled over it, and the legacy of Saint Markovia herself.

While exploring the north side of the village, you discovered a serene pool and a holy shrine; Kaz stepped forward and drank deeply from the blessed waters. The Burgomaster then introduced the party to a local named Balthazar, who provided your very first descriptive account of the bizarre "mongrelfolk" inhabiting the region. Suspecting foul play in the local cemetery, the party organized a midnight stakeout at the Burgomaster's house to catch a ring of suspected graverobbers. After midnight, your patience paid off as two shadowed figures crept toward the estate carrying shovels. The party ambushed and successfully captured them, revealing them to be two strange mongrelfolk. You secured the prisoners, slept through the remainder of the night to gain a long rest, and prepared to interrogate them in the morning.
