# Session 23: The Abbot's Bride

**Date:** October 23, 2024

The morning began with the interrogation of your two unusual prisoners, who identified themselves as Zygfrek and Otto Belview. They shared a tragic tale, explaining how the entire Belview family had been abducted from the village ten years prior by the mysterious Abbot. To verify their story, you escorted them to their old, abandoned family plot; the Burgomaster positively identified the siblings and confirmed ownership of the house.

Resolved to investigate the mountaintop monastery, the party headed up the steep path to the Abbey of Saint Markovia. Upon arrival, you sent Bloodwing to snoop around the perimeter to scout for danger. Pushing into the courtyard, you discovered dozens of deformed mongrelfolk locked away inside rows of wooden sheds.

Stepping into the main hall, you finally met the enigmatic Abbot alongside a quiet woman named Vasilka. Gillian immediately stepped forward and shared a bizarre, courtly dance with Vasilka. The Abbot openly explained his horrific project: he was meticulously sewing together a flesh-golem bride to present to Strahd in an attempt to satisfy the vampire's curse, pointing out her striking, uncanny resemblance to Tatyana. Realizing the absolute madness and horror of the situation, weapons were drawn and initiative was rolled!
