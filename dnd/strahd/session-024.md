# Session 24: Clash at the Abbey

**Date:** December 18, 2024

The confrontation at the Abbey began with a terrifying transformation as the Abbot shed his mortal guise, revealing himself to be a fallen angel adorned with massive, radiant wings. Simultaneously, the flesh-golem bride Vasilka let out a horrific, blood-curdling screech and lunged forward to attack the party.

The battle escalated instantly into a clash of titans when Gillian cast *polymorph* to transform himself into a colossal giant ape—only for the celestial Abbot to counter by morphing into a massive giant ape himself. A brutal wrestling match echoed through the stone hall as the party focused their fire on the angelic horror. Through coordinated strikes and immense physical force, you ultimately defeated the Abbot. The moment the celestial fell, the magic animating Vasilka collapsed, causing the golem to dramatically fall apart into a gruesome pile of dislodged body parts. With the primary threats neutralized, you cautiously ascended the grand staircase of the main hall, where you encountered a lone mongrelfolk casually playing music, sitting adjacent to a large, mysterious object concealed beneath a dusty cloth sheet.
