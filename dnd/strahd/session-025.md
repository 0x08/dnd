# Session 25: Madness in the Courtyard

**Date:** January 15, 2025

The party approached the musical mongrelfolk, who identified himself as Clovin Belview. Through careful diplomacy and a bit of trickery, you convinced him to head down into the Abbey's cellar to fetch more wine for the group. However, the moment Clovin descended into the darkness, you heard him screaming in pure terror as he bolted back out into the open courtyard. In the confusion, the Abbey bells were accidentally rung; the moment the iron knell echoed over the peaks, the entire population of locked-up mongrelfolk began screaming in a horrific, unified chorus of "Food, food!!"

The Abbey erupted into absolute chaos. The party moved swiftly through the rooms to clear out emerging threats. Wilbin playfully jumped onto a row of old beds, inadvertently disturbing a nest of malevolent shadows that swarmed out to attack, though you quickly destroyed them. While searching the upper chambers, you discovered a woman named Ezmerelda d'Avenir hidden in one of the rooms. She revealed that she was a renowned vampire hunter who had infiltrated the abbey while searching for her missing mentor, Rudolph van Richten. Recognizing a common cause, she agreed to rendezvous with the party back at the lightning tower at a later date.

Regrouping, you moved out into the central courtyard and killed a massive wave of frenzied mongrelfolk that had broken loose from the sheds. As you attempted to secure the eastern hallway, your progress was blocked by yet another massive flesh golem, backed by a fresh swarm of aggressive mongrelfolk.
