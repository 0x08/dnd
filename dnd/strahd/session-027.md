# Session 27: The Rescue of Arabelle

**Date:** March 11, 2025

The party traveled to the Vistani camp situated southwest of Vallaki, discovering a population of somber dusk elves living alongside the Vistani. You spoke at length with the dusk elves and gathered crucial lore about their tragic history. You learned of Patrina Velikov, sister to their current leader Kasimir Velikov, who had once been a promised bride to Strahd before she was murdered by her own people to prevent her damnation. In a fit of monstrous revenge, Strahd slaughtered every single woman and child among the dusk elves as punishment. Kasimir Velikov then spoke extensively about the Amber Temple, sharing his absolute certainty that the remote facility held the ultimate secret to Strahd's power, detailing how ancient wizards had locked away evil vestiges there long ago. He expressed a fierce desire to see the vampire lord utterly destroyed.

Turning your attention to the Vistani camp, you met a distraught leader named Luvash, who explained that his young daughter, Arabelle, had mysteriously vanished the previous day. Luvash handed you a doll that belonged to the girl to help aid your search. Utilizing a pack of trained mastiffs, you followed the scent of the doll all the way to the shores of Lake Baratok, north of Vallaki.

The party secured a couple of old rowing boats and paddled out onto the misty waters toward a lone figure fishing in the distance. As you drew near, Kaz spotted a little girl tightly bound in ropes resting at the bottom of the boat. The fisherman, a destitute local drunk named Bluto, suddenly panicked and threw the tied-up Arabelle into the deep water to drown as a sacrifice. Kaz reacted instantly, diving headfirst into the freezing lake and swimming downward to pull the sinking girl back to the surface. You successfully rescued Arabelle, who immediately and sassily demanded her doll back. The party returned the child safely to Luvash, who was profoundly grateful. In exchange for his daughter's life, Luvash offered the party full access to his trading wares, prompting you to draw up a shopping list for healing potions, diamonds for *revivify*, and other critical magical gear.
