# Session 28: Carriage to Castle Ravenloft

**Date:** March 25, 2025

The day began with Wilbin thoroughly searching the body of the captured Bluto, uncovering nothing but a half-empty bottle of cheap wine. With the reward and favor from Luvash secured, the party finalized their extensive shopping and trading lists. Kaz arranged to sell a collection of weapons including a greataxe, a longbow, a morningstar, and a shield for 50 gold pieces, while Ulvor sold an ornate scepter and Kevin traded away a set of golden hawks. In return, the party organized their dream acquisition list: Kaz sought a *clockwork amulet* and a *cloak of protection*; Kevin requested a *pearl of power* and an *amulet of the devout*; Ulvor selected a *ring of jumping*, a *cloak of protection*, and an assortment of high-tier healing potions; Wilbin opted for a *pearl of power*; and Gillian finalized his bardic magic items via a private message to Luvash.

Before leaving Vallaki, you made a final stop at Blinsky’s Toys to purchase some curiosities. The eccentric toymaker spoke about Rictavio, noting that the showman was a frequent visitor who had gifted him his pet monkey, and confirmed that Rictavio's carnival wagon was still parked in town. Blinsky also shared a deeply unsettling detail: another mysterious customer had been forcing him to construct custom dolls, and the latest batch was designed to look exactly like Ireena Kolyana. Finally, he shared rumors of a legendary "clock man" hidden somewhere within the walls of Castle Ravenloft, asking the party to keep an eye out for it.

With their preparations complete, the party set off toward the brooding silhouette of Castle Ravenloft. Upon reaching the dark crossroads, you found a grand black coach waiting in the mist with its doors swung open. The party climbed inside—with Ulvor boldly choosing to sit outside on the driver's bench—and the carriage automatically whisked you up the cliffs directly to the castle gates. Waiting in the courtyard was Strahd’s sinister chamberlain, Rahadin, who silently led the group inside to the grand dining room. There, an ominous cloaked figure sat with his back to you, playing a dramatic melody on a massive pipe organ as Rahadin coldly requested that the party take their seats.
