# Session 29: Dinner and Gargoyles

**Date:** April 29, 2025

The heavy wooden doors clicked shut and locked behind you as the party took their seats to begin dinner. The mysterious figure seated at the organ turned to address the group, cordially inviting them to enjoy their meal and engaging in brief, mocking banter. Suddenly, the figure dissolved into thin air with a mocking, cackling laugh, revealing that the host was merely an illusion. Realizing the trap had sprung, the party wasted no time and used brute force to break down the locked doors leading back out to the main hall.

As you navigated the grand corridors, Kevin spotted a set of fine plate armor standing against the wall and immediately began donning the heavy steel pieces. However, the castle defenses reacted instantly as a flock of vicious stone gargoyles detached themselves from the architecture and swooped down to attack.

The party fought their way through the hall and retreated out into the open courtyard to take a short rest and wait for logistical reinforcements. Lowering the heavy iron drawbridge, you allowed your faithful Shield Guardian, Shaloth, to march into the castle compound to deliver your primary weapons and equipment chest. Re-armed and ready for a dungeon crawl, you stepped back inside the castle proper, only to be instantly caught in a hidden floor trap that triggered a sudden architectural shift, instantly separating the party and teleporting Kaz and Ulvor away into the dark, unknown depths of area K47.
