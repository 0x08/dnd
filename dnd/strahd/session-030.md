# Session 30: The Beating Heart

**Date:** June 18, 2025

Separated from their allies, Wilbin, Gillian, and Kevin carefully advanced up a grand spiral staircase in search of the missing fighters. Further up the flights, they successfully reunited with Kaz and Ulvor, who were found standing in a daze, staring blankly at a haunting, enchanted portrait of Strahd von Zarovich. As the group consolidated, Wilbin noticed a fine carpet on the floor and attempted to lift it; the textile instantly surged to life as an animated rug of smothering, wrapping itself tightly around Wilbin and swallowing him whole. Simultaneously, the magical portrait flared to life, temporarily seizing telepathic control over Ulvor's mind. The party tore the rug to pieces and snapped Ulvor out of his trance.

Pressing forward, you ascended to the absolute peak of the stone stairs, coming face-to-face with a grand and terrifying sight: a massive, crystalline red heart floating in the center of the North Tower, beating with an eerie, rhythmic pulse. Deciding to explore lower levels before tackling the artifact, you navigated back down the spiral stairway.

You pushed through a bizarre room filled with dozens of stray cats, followed by a dark laboratory lined with shelves of glowing glass vials. There, a coven of wicked castle witches ambushed the group. The party fought fiercely, killing the hags and looting seven homebrew magical potions from their workstations. Driven by reckless curiosity, Wilbin immediately uncorked and drank one of the unknown mixtures, instantly poisoning himself. Groaning and recovering from the toxins, the party made their way further down the stairs, stepping cautiously into the castle's grand foyer.
