# Session 31: The Catacombs Entrance

**Date:** September 16, 2025

The party officially transitioned onto the Virtual Tabletop (VTT) grid to navigate the complex lower architecture of the castle. As you crept through the shadows, you crossed paths with Escher, a sophisticated vampire consort of Strahd. Rather than attacking, he casually engaged the group and led you down into a massive bone room dominated by a colossal dragon skull mounted to the wall.

Turning south to scout the perimeter, the party was suddenly ambushed by ten ancient skeletons lurking in the darkness; you formed a defensive line and swiftly crushed the undead bones to dust. Turning north, you discovered a hidden secret passage that descended into a grand chamber housing a large, magical brazier surrounded by colored stones. Adjacent to the brazier room, you found a viewing platform holding two lonely chairs overlooking a vast abyss. Pushing down a narrow, damp passageway, Kevin stepped squarely onto a hidden pressure plate, instantly triggering a trap that dropped a heavy iron gate and securely locked him inside a cramped solitary cell. The rest of the party scrambled to block the remaining mechanism, desperately searching for a way around the architecture to free their trapped ally.
