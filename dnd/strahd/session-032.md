# Session 32: Crypts and Vampire Spawn

**Date:** October 13, 2025

The session was spent executing a frantic rescue mission to extract Kevin from his solitary cell. The party initially entered the vast castle catacombs to find an alternate route, but your path was blocked by rows of sealed tombs, noting a single, solitary coffin resting in a prominent alcove. Realizing the crypts didn't connect directly, you navigated through a series of flooded tunnels, utilizing the water channels to finally bypass the iron bars and reach Kevin's cell.

However, the rescue quickly devolved into magical chaos as Kaz stepped on a teleportation rune and was instantly zapped away into a separate locked cell; after freeing him, he managed to step on the rune a second time, instantly teleporting himself right back into confinement. During the chaotic exploration of the damp chambers, you uncovered a valuable relic: the *Crusader's Shortsword*.

After gathering the party back together, you approached the solitary coffin you had spotted earlier and pried it open, finding nothing inside but a luxurious, empty purple velvet lining. Seeking a way out, you pulled a heavy wall lever, which dramatically lowered a massive iron portcullis elsewhere in the dungeon. The moment the mechanism clicked into place, an ambush was sprung as three bloodthirsty vampire spawn materialized from the shadows, forcing the party into a desperate melee combat.
