# Session 34: The Light of Argynvostholt

**Date:** January 2026

The party returned to the grand bone room to retrieve the massive artifact they had discovered earlier. The moment you stepped into the chamber, a horde of castle horrors ambushed the group, triggering a frantic skirmish. During the opening round of combat, Alex took a massive blow and was completely knocked unconscious. The party rallied around their fallen ally, unleashing a barrage of divine and physical strikes that successfully dispatched the monsters. Once the chamber was secure and Alex was healed back to consciousness, you stepped up to the wall and used your combined strength to pry the massive dragon skull off its mounts.

While completing a final sweep of the immediate area, you successfully identified two more powerful items: the *Icon of Ravenloft* and a magical *mace*. Strapping the heavy dragon skull to the tireless shoulders of your Shield Guardian, Shaloth, the party executed a daring escape from Castle Ravenloft.

You marched through the gloomy Barovian wilderness all the way back to the ruined manor of Argynvostholt. Entering the grand estate, you carried the massive skull up to the central tower and returned the remains of the silver dragon to their rightful resting place. Instantly, a brilliant, supernatural beacon of pure, silvery light flared to life atop the manor's tower, piercing through the eternal mists and casting a ray of profound hope across the entire valley.
