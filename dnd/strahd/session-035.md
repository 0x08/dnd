# Session 35: Tiger in the Stockyard

**Date:** March 16, 2026

With the inspiring beacon of Argynvostholt now burning bright in the sky, the party marched back to the Vistani camp southwest of Vallaki to cash in their favors and collect the gear they had previously negotiated. The trade was highly successful: you officially took possession of a *pearl of power*, a *cloak of protection*, a set of magical pipes, and a substantial crate of healing potions.

From the camp, you traveled into the town of Vallaki and checked back into the Blue Water Inn. That evening, Gillian put on an absolute masterclass performance in the tavern room. The show caught the attention of the mysterious Rictavio, who watched from the corner, thoroughly impressed by the bard's skill.

After closing time, you quietly followed Rictavio out to the town stockyard as he approached his heavily reinforced carnival wagon. Creeping up to the wooden bars, you spoke directly with the massive sabre-tooth tiger locked inside using *speak with animals*. The beast shared a chilling detail: it had been meticulously trained to hunt and kill Vistani, whom it described as "the direct servants of the absolute evil that controls these dark lands." Leaving Ulvor behind to keep a watchful eye on the stockyard, the rest of the party headed back to their rooms at the inn, formulating a plan to confront Rictavio directly over breakfast before securing a long rest.
