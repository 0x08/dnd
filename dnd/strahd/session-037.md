# Session 37: The Witch of the Swamp

**Date:** May 6, 2026

The infiltration quickly turned into a deadly trap; Baba Lysaga was not caught off guard, but had been hiding magically in the surrounding swamp, waiting for the characters to step inside her threshold. The moment the party entered the structure, the ancient witch surged out from the briars and clambered into a massive, floating giant's skull. Flying rapidly up into the storm clouds, she cackled loudly and unleashed the full fury of her high-level arcane repertoire upon the group.

The battlefield became an absolute nightmare of high-tier magic. Baba Lysaga rained down devastating spells, rocking the party with *power word stun*, a lethal green ray of *finger of death*, and a suffocating cloud of *cloudkill* that blanketed the swamp water. Simultaneously, the ground churned as she summoned massive swarms of biting insects and a battalion of terrifying, animated scarecrows that marched out from the reeds to surround the bogged-down party.
