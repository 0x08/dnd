    Session 3: The Fall of Vogler

    As the vanguard of the Dragon Army began pounding against the village defenses, Boycott slipped away to Thornwall Keep to fulfill the urgent task Becklin had requested. Sneaking into her private quarters, Boycott located the hidden box beneath the frame. Peeking inside, he discovered a magnificent, pristine suit of Solamnic plate armor, but opted to leave it secure in its hiding place for the time being. Looking out from the high windows of the keep, he watched in horror as the main body of the invading army came marching down the highway like a crimson wave. Boycott sprinted back through the cobblestone streets to rejoin the main defense force.

    Just as he arrived, a battered mercenary came sprinting through the lines to deliver a dire report: the invading host had split into a deadly two-pronged assault, with one faction driving hard for the docks to cut off the escape boats while the other smashed into the mercenary lines. Before the warning could even register, a wing of terrifying, winged monsters known as Draconians swooped out of the smoky sky to attack. The party coordinated beautifully, utilizing swift steel and arcane power to quickly dispatch the reptilian beasts.

    The retreat quickly devolved into a desperate, shifting urban warzone. You cut your way through successive waves of invading Draconians, pausing in shock as you spotted three mysterious, heavily armored knights watching the carnage from the clifftops before vanishing into thin air. Amidst the chaos of the burning buildings, a stray, terrified cat leaped directly onto Snub's chest, clinging to his armor with white-knuckled grip; Snub instantly fell in love, naming the brave feline Marmaduke, and the two became utterly inseparable for the remainder of the campaign.

    The party pushed through the smoke toward the wharf, where the village was being completely overrun. There, a final defensive line of Draconians had deployed a terrifying piece of gnomish siege machinery known as a Boilerdrak, which spewed torrents of liquid flame across the docks. You launched a desperate assault, cutting down the operators and disabling the infernal machine. Just as the final civilian transports were casting off, the brutal commander of the vanguard, Fewmaster Gholcag, stormed the wharf. A brutal, bloody boss battle erupted, leaving multiple party members unconscious and bleeding out on the wooden planks. Through sheer determination and clutch healing, you rallied and brought the warlord crashing down.

    With the village thoroughly overrun and completely engulfed in flames, you leaped onto the very last boat casting off from the shore. As the skiff drifted into the current, the mercenary captain Jeyev came splashing through the shallows, clambering aboard with a shattered officer's helmet clutched tightly in his hands. Together, you floated down the river, watching in heartbroken silence as Vogler burned to ash behind you. Through this baptism of fire, your shared hardships forged you into true heroes, and you officially achieved level 4!
