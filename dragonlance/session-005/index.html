    Session 5: The Ashes of the Frontier

    Upon your victorious return to the gates of Kalaman alongside Tatina Rookledust, your first order of business was escorting your sole goblin prisoner, affectionately dubbed Jim the Goblin, down into the high-security cells beneath Kalaman Castle. With your duty fulfilled for the day, the party headed into the city's entertainment district to spend a long, celebratory night drinking heavily at the popular Wanderer’s Rest tavern. Amidst the rowdy atmosphere, you struck up a conversation with a local blacksmith named Gareth, who shared a troubling rumor regarding a local fisherman named John who operated a vessel called the Mayflower. According to Gareth, John had recently been offered a massive sum of gold by mysterious agents to enlist as a high-ranking recruit within the Dragon Army.

    The remainder of the evening devolved into pure, unadulterated revelry alongside the surprisingly wild Tatina Rookledust. The ale flowed freely, and at a certain point in the night, members of the party were even seen dancing wildly atop the tavern tables to the cheers of the patrons.

    The inevitable consequence arrived the following morning in the form of a massive, pounding hangover for the entire taskforce. The pain was quickly cut short as Darrett arrived with an urgent deployment: a panicked farming family had just fled into the city gates, reporting that their frontier estate had been seized by an advanced scouting element of the Dragon Army. Following Darrett's precise tactical orders, you shook off the alcohol and marched out to the frontier farmstead.

    Arriving at the property, you encountered a ruthless Dragon Army officer backed by a disciplined squad of six fierce Hobgoblins who were busy fortifying the farmhouse. A fierce, tactical skirmish ensued. During the fighting, you successfully captured a heavily scarred hobgoblin whom you securely bound and labeled "Jobbo the Burned." The party eliminated the officer and the remaining troops, but during the frantic melee, the structure was accidentally set ablaze, burning the farmhouse to the ground. Fortunately, the family's brave hound, Nono the dog, managed to escape the flames completely unharmed, bringing a bright spot of joy to the grim afternoon. Through this successful defense of the frontier, the party's growing combat synergy pushed them to a major milestone, and you officially attained level 5!

    On the road back to Kalaman, you were surprised to cross paths with a familiar face: Cudgel Ironclad. The battle-weary mercenary commander explained that she and her tiny accompanying escort were quite literally all that remained of her legendary Ironclad Regiment. Following their catastrophic defeat at the fall of Vogler, they had been forced to navigate a grueling, winding route through the wilderness to evade enemy scouts and make it back to civilization.

    Upon returning to headquarters, you immediately met with Marshal Vendri to deliver your report. Recognizing the growing proximity of the threat, the Marshal agreed to significantly increase the city's frontier defenses, provided the party immediately march out to investigate and scout a critical choke point known as Wheelwatch Outpost. Darrett was instructed to prepare all the necessary maps and tactical briefs for a dawn deployment.
