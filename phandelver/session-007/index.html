    Session 7: The Ambush on the Trail

    The orkish masquerade dissolved into absolute violence the moment you stepped into the inner sanctum. The party launched a devastating surprise assault, slaying the orc chieftain Brughor Axebiter and his towering pet ogre, Gog. You severed the raiders' heads to serve as proof of the deed and traveled safely back to Conyberry for a brief rest before steering the horse cart back down the Triboar Trail toward Phandalin.

    However, mid-way through the journey, the forest went completely silent as a high-stakes ambush was sprung. Stepping out from the treeline to block the road were Glassstaff and the legendary mastermind himself, the Black Spider. The dross villain raised his hand, delivering a chilling psychic message directly into your minds, gloating that your efforts to stop him from claiming Wave Echo Cave were entirely worthless and that his minions would collect your heads. The moment the monologue concluded, the Black Spider swigged a magical potion of invisibility and completely vanished from the battlefield.

    A frantic, deadly skirmish erupted in the center of the road. Glassstaff unleashed a barrage of arcane missiles while backed by three massive, chitinous giant spiders that lunged at the horses. The party fought with exceptional coordination, crushing the giant arachnids and successfully subduing Glassstaff before he could escape a second time. Searching the defeated wizard, Gillian proudly claimed his signature glass staff as a prize of war. Through this monumental victory over the campaign's primary antagonists, the party's growing capabilities reached an elite threshold, and you officially attained level 4!

    You escorted the horse cart safely back into Phandalin. Your first stop was Sister Garaele’s shrine, but the priestess was deeply annoyed to find that Gillian had wasted her magical question on personal vanity instead of asking about the legendary spellbook; she presented the party with a cold shoulder and dismissed you. Undeterred, you marched to the Townmaster’s hall to successfully claim the heavy gold bounty on the Wyvern Tor orcs. Sildar Hallwinter warmly congratulated the group on defeating Glassstaff, but strongly urged you to immediate prioritize finding Cragmaw Castle to rescue the kidnapped Gundren Rockseeker.
