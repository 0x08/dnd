    Session 33: The Luck Blade and Royal Tombs

    Date: December 16, 2025

    Following the destruction of the vampire spawn, the party began a systematic exploration of the ancient castle crypts, opening several sealed tombs. One crypt was found to contain the skeletal remains of a legendary gnomish mage named Gralmore Nimblenobs; Wilbin carefully searched the dust and recovered a valuable bundle of arcane spell scrolls. Seeking to activate another mechanism, Wilbin inserted his brass-ended staff into a strange wall receptacle, triggering an immediate magical feedback loop that heavily zapped him with electricity. Undeterred, you opened an adjacent royal vault and fought off Strahd's legendary fiendish nightmare steed, Beucephalus, successfully slaying the hellish beast.

    Utilizing detect magic to screen the remaining tombs, the party uncovered an incredible trove of legendary relics. Inside one crypt, you discovered a functional magical portal rune. In another, you hit a massive milestone: you uncovered a legendary Luck Blade shortsword, which still held exactly one reality-altering wish charge. Continuing your sweep, you discovered Saint Markovia's Thighbone (a potent weapon against the undead) and a complete Deck of Illusions.

    Pushing deep into the absolute heart of the catacombs, you located the grand, somber tombs of King Barov von Zarovich and Queen Ravenovia van Roeyen. Finally, the party breached the sealed tomb of Sergei von Zarovich, where you discovered a pristine, magnificent set of magical plate armor resting atop his stone effigy.
