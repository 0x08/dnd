    Session 36: The Ruins of Berez

    Date: April 29, 2026

    The next morning, the party sat down with Rictavio over a hearty breakfast at the inn and confronted him about his secret wagon. Cornered by your intelligence, the showman dropped his disguise and proudly revealed his true identity: he was indeed the legendary vampire hunter, Rudolph van Richten. You spent the morning discussing Strahd’s tactical weaknesses, and Van Richten gave a solemn vow that he would stand ready to join the party when the final moment came to confront the vampire lord at Castle Ravenloft.

    With a powerful ally secured, the party marched south to deal with the remaining threat in the valley: Baba Lysaga. You arrived at the gloomy, swampy ruins of Berez and began a thorough exploration of the abandoned settlement. You inspected a crumbling, dilapidated mansion, finding nothing but rows of empty wine casks rotting away in the damp cellar.

    Turning your attention toward the center of the marsh, you approached a bizarre, giant hut surrounded by rusted cages filled with screaming, flapping marsh birds. Working quickly, you managed to pry the cages open to free the birds before stepping forward and boldly opening the front door to Baba Lysaga's creeping domain.
